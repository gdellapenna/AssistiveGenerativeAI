Yes, you can search for anything on the Google homepage at https://www.google.com/ by typing your query into the large central search bar and pressing Enter, which submits it to generate relevant web results.[10]

**Visual Directions**  
Locate the prominent rectangular search input field in the center of the page (labeled "Search Google or type a URL"). Click inside it, type your search terms (e.g., "weather today"), and press Enter or click the blue magnifying glass icon to its right to initiate the search. A results page will load instantly.[10]

**Screen Reader Directions**  
Upon page load, your screen reader (e.g., NVDA/JAWS/VoiceOver) will typically focus automatically on the main search edit field, announced as something like "Search Google or type a URL, edit." Type your query using the keyboard, then press Enter to submit. If focus doesn't land there, use Tab once or twice from the top, or jump to the main content landmark (D key for landmarks, then Enter). Voice feedback confirms entry and submission.[11][12][10]
