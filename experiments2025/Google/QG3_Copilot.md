On the **Google homepage (https://www.google.com/)**, there is **no direct link labeled “Google News.”** However, you can still access Google News from this page by performing a search or typing directly into the search box.

---

## 👀 Visual Navigation (Sighted User)
1. In the **center of the page**, locate the large **search box** under the Google logo.  
2. Type **Google News** into the search box.  
3. Press **Enter** on your keyboard, or click the **“Google Search”** button just below the box.  
4. The results page will appear. The **first result** is usually the official Google News site.  
5. Click that link to go to Google News.

---

## 🎧 Screen Reader Navigation (Blind User)
1. When the page loads, press **Tab once** (or use your screen reader’s “Form field navigation” command) to move focus to the **search input field**.  
2. Type **Google News**.  
3. Press **Enter**. This activates the **Google Search** function.  
4. Your screen reader will announce the search results page.  
5. Use the **Tab key** or your screen reader’s **Links List** command to navigate to the **first link**, which is typically the official Google News site.  
6. Press **Enter** to open it.

---

✅ **Summary:** You cannot directly access Google News from the homepage with a single link, but you can reach it by typing “Google News” into the search box and following the first result.
