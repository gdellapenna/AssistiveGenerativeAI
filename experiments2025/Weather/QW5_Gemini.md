## 🔎 Searching for a Specific Location Forecast

### Visual Instructions

1.  Look at the top of the page where the logo and main navigation are located.
2.  Identify the input field, which is usually a long, prominent bar labeled with placeholder text such as **"Search for a City or Zip Code."**
3.  **Click** inside this search bar.
4.  **Type** the name of the city, town, or the zip code you are interested in.
5.  As you type, a list of suggested locations will appear below the bar. **Click** on the correct location in that list to immediately load the forecast.

### Screen Reader Instructions

1.  When the page loads, use the **"E" key** (or the command to jump to the next **"Edit"** field). This should take you directly to the **"Search for a City or Zip Code"** text input.
2.  Your screen reader will announce that you are on an **"Edit"** or **"Text Input"** field.
3.  **Type** your desired location (e.g., "London" or "90210").
4.  Listen carefully, as the screen reader will often announce the appearance of a **"Listbox"** or **"Suggestions."**
5.  Use the **Down Arrow key** to navigate through the suggested locations in the list.
6.  When your screen reader announces the correct location, press **Enter** to select it. This action submits your search and loads the new forecast page.

**Tip:** If you prefer, after typing the location, you can also press the **Tab key** once to move to the **"Search Button"** (often a magnifying glass icon) and press **Enter** there to submit the location.
