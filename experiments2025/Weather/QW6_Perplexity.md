The weather.com website allows you to switch to another language using a language selection feature located near the search bar on the homepage.

Visual Directions to Change Language:
1. At the top of the homepage, near the search box, look for a globe icon or a button indicating language or region settings.
2. Select or click this globe icon to open the language selection menu.
3. A list of available languages will appear. Navigate through the list to find the language you want to switch to.
4. Select your preferred language from the list.
5. The page will reload or update automatically to display content in the chosen language.

Screen Reader Directions:
1. After the page loads, use your screen reader to navigate to the region near the search input at the top of the page.
2. Locate the button or link announced as "language selector," "change language," or associated with the globe icon.
3. Activate this button using Enter or the screen reader click command to open the language menu.
4. Use arrow keys or navigation commands to move through the language options presented in the list.
5. Press Enter on your chosen language.
6. Wait for the page to refresh or update; your screen reader will then announce content in the new language.

This process is the official method on weather.com for switching languages and it supports multiple regional and language options. It also often includes selecting region-specific content and measurement units like Celsius or Fahrenheit.

If you need detailed step-by-step help in navigating the language menu or selecting a particular language, that assistance can be provided.
