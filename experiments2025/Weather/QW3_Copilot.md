To access the **10-day weather forecast** on weather.com, here’s how you can do it:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users

1. **Locate the top navigation bar** near the top of the page.
2. Find and **click the link labeled “10 Day”**. It’s positioned between “Hourly” and “Radar”.
3. This will take you to a new page showing the daily forecast for the next 10 days, including temperature highs/lows, precipitation, and conditions.

---

### 🎧 For Screen Reader Users

1. **Navigate to the top of the page** using your screen reader’s “top of document” command.
2. Use the **“next link”** or **“tab”** command until you hear “Link 10 Day”.
3. **Activate the link** using Enter or your screen reader’s click command.
4. The new page will load with a heading like “10 Day Weather Forecast”. Use **heading navigation** (H1–H3) or **table navigation** to explore each day’s forecast.
