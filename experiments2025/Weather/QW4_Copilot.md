Yes, you can access radar maps directly from the weather.com homepage. Here's how:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users

1. **Go to the top navigation bar** near the top of the page.
2. Look for the link labeled **“Radar”**. It’s located between “10 Day” and “Video”.
3. **Click “Radar”** to open the radar maps section. This will take you to a new page with interactive weather radar maps showing precipitation, storms, and other conditions.

---

### 🎧 For Screen Reader Users

1. **Navigate to the top of the page** using your screen reader’s “top of document” command.
2. Use the **“next link”** or **“tab”** command until you hear “Link Radar”.
3. **Activate the link** using Enter or your screen reader’s click command.
4. The new page will load with radar maps. Use **heading navigation** to find map descriptions or controls, and **form or region navigation** to interact with map filters or settings if available.
