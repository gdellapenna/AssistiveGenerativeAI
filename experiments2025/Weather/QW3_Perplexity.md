To access the 10-day weather forecast on the weather.com homepage, follow these steps:

Visual Directions:
1. Locate the main navigation menu at the top of the page.
2. Among the menu options, find the link labeled "10 Day."
3. Click or select "10 Day" to open the detailed 10-day weather forecast page for the location currently displayed or selected.

Screen Reader Directions:
1. After the page loads, use your screen reader's navigation commands to move to the main navigation region near the top.
2. Navigate through the menu links until you find the link labeled "10 Day."
3. Press Enter or activate it using your screen reader to access the 10-day weather forecast.
4. The resulting page or panel will provide detailed weather information for each of the next ten days, including temperature highs and lows, precipitation chances, wind, and conditions.

This menu option is part of the main horizontal menu bar prominently placed at the top of the site and is designed for quick access to extended forecasts. It is a straightforward way to get a detailed outlook beyond the current day or hourly updates.
