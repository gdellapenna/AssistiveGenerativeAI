* The link/button labeled **"Aumenta carattere"** to make the font larger.

**Visual Directions:**
Look for the links near the very top of the page:
* **"Versione grafica"** (Graphic version)
* **"Solo testo"** (Text only)

**Screen Reader Directions:**

* The link labeled **"Solo testo"** to switch to a plain text version, which eliminates complex graphics and styling, often improving readability for screen reader users and those requiring simpler contrast.
