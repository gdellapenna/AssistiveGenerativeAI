Yes, this page provides contact information and methods under several sections, primarily in the footer and quick access links.

Here is how to find the contact details:

### 1. General Contact Information (Address and P.IVA)

**Visual Directions:**
Scroll all the way down to the **footer** of the page (the very bottom). The main address and tax information are listed here.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the **footer** of the page (usually the last section). You will find the following static text:

* **Università degli Studi dell'Aquila**
* **Palazzo Camponeschi, piazza Santa Margherita 2, 67100 L'Aquila** (The physical address)
* **P.IVA-IBAN** (Tax ID/VAT and bank information)

### 2. Dedicated Contact Links (URP, Contatti, PEC)

The footer also contains specific links for different types of contact:

**Visual Directions:**
In the final section of the footer, look for a group of links that includes **URP** and **Contatti**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
In the footer, look for the following three specific **links**:

* **URP**: This stands for *Ufficio Relazioni con il Pubblico* (Public Relations Office), which is a general contact point.
* **Contatti**: This is the generic link for **Contacts**. Activating this link would likely take you to a dedicated contact page with phone numbers and email addresses.
* **PEC**: This is a link related to the university's Certified Electronic Mail address (a specific type of email required by law in Italy for official communications).

### 3. Quick Access to Directory

There is also a link in the **Quick Access Menu** that functions as a staff directory:

**Visual Directions:**
Near the top of the page, in the "Accesso veloce" list, look for the link "Chi e dove."

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the **"Accesso veloce"** (Quick Access) links at the top. Find and activate the link labeled:

* **Chi e dove** (Who and where / Directory) - This link would lead you to a searchable directory of university personnel and offices.
