On the home page of Università degli Studi dell’Aquila (UNIVAQ) — [https://www.univaq.it](https://www.univaq.it) — there isn’t a single “Enroll now” button. Enrollment (immatricolazione) is handled through **specific sections of the site**, depending on whether you are a first-year student (newly admitted) or a continuing student. Here’s how you can navigate there, step by step.

---

## 🔎 Visual navigation (sighted user)

1. Open the home page: `https://www.univaq.it`.
2. At the top main menu, locate **“Futura matricola”** — this is for prospective first-year students.
3. Click on **“Futura matricola”**. You will arrive at a page specifically for new students, with information about enrollment procedures, requirements, and links to online enrollment forms.
4. Follow the links on that page for the **online enrollment platform**. This usually involves:

   * Choosing the degree course you want to enroll in (e.g., Computer Science).
   * Filling in personal information, academic records, and uploading required documents.
   * Paying the enrollment fee.
5. If you are a continuing student (not first-year), you would instead go to **“Studente”** in the main menu, then look for the enrollment or re-registration links for returning students.

---

## 🔉 Screen‑reader friendly steps

1. Open `https://www.univaq.it`. Wait for page load; the first heading or link should be read.
2. Use your screen reader’s **link list** or **tab through links** until you find **“Futura matricola”**.
3. Press Enter on that link. You are now in the “Future Students” section. The heading should announce “Futura matricola” or “Prospective Students.”
4. Use headings or link navigation to locate information about enrollment (“Iscrizione / Immatricolazione”). There will typically be:

   * A link to the online enrollment system (often labeled “Procedura di immatricolazione online”).
   * Information about deadlines, required documents, and fees.
5. Follow the instructions on that page: select your degree programme, complete forms, and submit.
