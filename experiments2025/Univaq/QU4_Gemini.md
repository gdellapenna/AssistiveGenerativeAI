## Screen Reader Navigation

To find this information using a screen reader:

1.  Navigate to the **Primary Site Menu**.
2.  Activate the link labeled **"Didattica"**.
3.  Use your reader's commands (like "Read Next Item" or "Navigate List") to read through the list of submenu **links** that appears, starting from **"Dipartimenti"** down to **"Link utili"**.
