---

## 🖥️ Visual navigation (mouse/trackpad)

1. Look at the **main navigation bar** just below the university logo.  
2. Hover over **“Ateneo”**.  
   - This menu typically contains information about the university structure, departments, and staff.  
3. Alternatively, hover over **“Servizi”**.  
   - This may include links to directories or services where you can search for people.  
4. If you see an item like **“Rubrica”** (Directory) or **“Contatti”**, click it.  
   - That section will allow you to look up staff or offices.

1. Use the **Tab key** or navigate by links until the screen reader announces “Ateneo link.”  
2. Press **Enter** to expand the submenu.  
3. Move through the submenu items with arrow keys or Tab.  
   - Listen for items like “Rubrica” (Directory) or “Contatti.”  
4. Press **Enter** on one of these links.  
   - This will open the directory or contact page where you can search for someone.
