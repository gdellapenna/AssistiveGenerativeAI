To reach the university course catalogue on the University of L'Aquila website, you interact with the menus as follows:

Visible interaction:
1. On the homepage or any page, locate the main menu bar at the top.
2. Find and select the menu item labeled "Didattica" (which means Teaching or Academic Offer).
3. Within the "Didattica" submenu or page, look for the link labeled "Course catalogue" or "Catalogo corsi."
4. Click this link to open the Course Catalogue web application.
5. Here, you can browse courses by degree level (bachelor's, master's, etc.), enrollment year, or search for specific programs with a search tool (lens icon) on the top right.

Screen reader interaction:
1. After the page loads, press **Tab** repeatedly until the screen reader announces "Didattica" in the main menu, then press **Enter**.
2. Use **Tab** or heading navigation to move through submenu links.
3. When you hear "Course catalogue" or "Catalogo corsi," activate the link by pressing **Enter**.
4. Once in the Course Catalogue, use Tab and arrow keys to navigate programs, search fields, and course details.
5. You can switch language from Italian to English via a language toggle (IT/EN) usually near the top.

This catalogue provides detailed information about all university study programs, their objectives, admission requirements, curricula, study plans, and syllabuses for each academic year.[1][2]
