On the UNIVAQ home page ([https://www.univaq.it](https://www.univaq.it)), the site supports **English** in addition to Italian. Here’s how to switch languages:

---

## 🔎 Visual navigation (sighted user)

1. Open the home page.
2. Look at the **top-right corner of the page** — there should be a small link or button labelled **“EN”** (for English) or **“IT / EN”**.
3. Click **“EN”** to switch the site to English. The page reloads in English, including main menus and key content.
4. To switch back to Italian, click **“IT”** in the same location.

---

## 🔉 Screen‑reader friendly steps

1. Open `https://www.univaq.it`. Wait for the page to load.
2. Use your screen reader’s **link list** or **tab through the top links**. Listen for a link with the text **“EN”** (for English).
3. When you hear “EN,” press Enter. The page reloads in English.
4. To return to Italian, repeat the process and activate the “IT” link.
