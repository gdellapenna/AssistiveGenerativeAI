To enroll at the University of L'Aquila, you should follow these steps:

1. **Find your program** on the university’s official website or course catalogue by browsing or searching for the degree you want to pursue.

2. **Register an account** on the university’s online application portal (Segreteria virtuale) using your personal data.

3. **Complete the online application form** by filling out your personal information, educational background, and selecting the program of your choice.

4. **Upload required documents**, which typically include transcripts or academic certificates, a motivation letter, curriculum vitae (CV), proof of English proficiency if applicable, and any program-specific documents.

5. **Submit your application** before the deadlines (application opening generally from November 25, 2024, and closing on March 5, 2025).

6. **Wait for admission notification** via email after the university reviews your application.

7. If accepted, **confirm your enrollment** within the deadlines provided, typically through the Segreteria virtuale platform by paying any required fees and completing enrollment forms.

8. After enrollment confirmation, follow any further steps like visa application (for international students), course registration, and orientation.

Using the website menus to enroll:
- Navigate to the "Segreteria virtuale" or "Student Secretary" section.
- Log in or create an account.
- Choose the application or enrollment option.
- Fill out forms, upload documents, and submit your application.

This process is supported by detailed guides and application timelines provided on the university's website to help you throughout the enrollment.

For a blind user with a screen reader:
- Use Tab to find and open "Segreteria virtuale."
- Log in or create an account as instructed.
- Use Tab and arrow keys to navigate through application forms.
- Use screen reader commands to read all instructions and fill in required fields.
- Upload documents via file upload controls.
- Submit and wait for email confirmation.

By carefully following these steps, you ensure successful enrollment at the University of L'Aquila for the 2025 academic year.[1][2][7]
