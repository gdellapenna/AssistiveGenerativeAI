The University of L'Aquila website reports several important deadlines and academic calendar dates for 2025, including:

- October 31, 2025, is a key deadline for submitting online enrollment applications, enrollment for second year and following years for free access courses, applications for enrollment in single classes, study plans, outgoing transfers, and other administrative requests.
- The academic calendar includes:
  - Beginning of didactic activities around October 20, 2025.
  - Winter term ends around early February 2026.
  - Spring term begins late February 2026.
  - Scheduled examination periods in winter and summer.
  - Holiday breaks such as Christmas (December 23 to January 6) and Easter (early April).
- For graduation, there are specific deadlines for thesis title approval between mid-July and October, thesis file upload deadlines from mid-September to December, and application windows for graduation.
- There are also scholarship and admission application deadlines such as March 5, 2025, and July 2, 2025, for Master's degrees taught in English.

These deadlines are detailed on pages related to the teaching calendar and student services, reflecting enrollment, exams, graduation, and admission timelines important for students at the university.
