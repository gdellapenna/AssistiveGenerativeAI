The University has two different areas on the page that relate to "secretary" services, depending on whether you are a current student or need general administration services.

Here are the places where you can click to access these sections:

### 1. For General Student Administration (Segreteria virtuale)

This link takes you to the main online student office.

**Visual Directions:**
Look near the top of the page, in the **"Servizi online"** (Online Services) list. Click on the link labeled **"Segreteria virtuale"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the section labeled **"Servizi online"**. Find and activate the link labeled **"Segreteria virtuale"** (Virtual Secretary/Student Office).

### 2. For Physical Student Offices (Segreterie studenti)

This link likely directs you to information regarding the physical locations and contacts for student offices.

**Visual Directions:**
Look in the **"Accesso veloce"** (Quick Access) menu near the top of the page. Click on the link labeled **"Segreterie studenti"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the list of links under **"Accesso veloce"**. Find and activate the link labeled **"Segreterie studenti"** (Student Offices).

### 3. As a Current Student (Studenti/Students)

You can also find a link to services for students under the "Servizi online" list. While not explicitly named "secretary," this link would take current students to their specific service portal, which often includes secretarial functions.

**Visual Directions:**
Look in the **"Servizi online"** (Online Services) list. Click on the link labeled **"Studenti"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the section labeled **"Servizi online"**. Find and activate the link labeled **"Studenti"**.
