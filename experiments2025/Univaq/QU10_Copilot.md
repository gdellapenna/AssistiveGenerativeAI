On the **current homepage of Università degli Studi dell’Aquila**, the enrollment process is not explained directly on the front page, but the menus guide you to the right section. The path is through the **Didattica** menu, where you’ll find the student secretariat and enrollment information.

---

## 🖥️ Visual navigation (mouse/trackpad)

1. Look at the **main navigation bar** just below the university logo.  
2. Hover your mouse over **“Didattica”**.  
3. In the dropdown menu, click on **“Segreterie studenti”** (Student secretaries).  
   - This section contains information about enrollment, registration, and administrative procedures.  
4. Once inside “Segreterie studenti,” look for links such as **“Immatricolazioni”** (Enrollments/Registrations) or **“Iscrizioni”** (Admissions).  
   - These links will guide you step by step through the enrollment process.

---

## 🎧 Screen reader navigation (keyboard)

1. Use the **Tab key** or navigate by links until the screen reader announces “Didattica link.”  
2. Press **Enter** to expand the submenu.  
3. Move through the submenu items with arrow keys or Tab until you hear “Segreterie studenti link.”  
4. Press **Enter** to activate it.  
   - The student secretary page will load.  
5. Navigate by headings or links until you hear “Immatricolazioni” or “Iscrizioni.”  
   - Press **Enter** on one of these links to access the enrollment instructions.

---
