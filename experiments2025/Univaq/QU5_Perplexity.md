Yes, the University of L'Aquila website does explain how to contact the university. There is a dedicated "Contact us" page that provides contact details for academic personnel as well as technical and administrative staff. It includes information on how to get support through the Help Point if you cannot find what you are looking for or if you have comments or questions about the site. The page also lists specific email addresses for support with online student services, technical problems, and navigation issues.

Practical contact details from the website include:
- Telephone numbers for the university main offices.
- Email addresses such as webmaster@univaq.it for website issues and servizi.online@univaq.it for student online service support.
- A general recommendation to write to the Help Point for support and general inquiries.
- Address details for the university location in L'Aquila, Italy.

***

How to find this information using a screen reader:
- From the homepage, press **Tab** to reach the main menu and navigate to "Contacts" or “Contatti” and press **Enter**.
- On the "Contact us" page, use Tab or heading navigation to find sections with phone numbers, email addresses, and support instructions.
- Phone numbers and email links will be announced and can be activated with **Enter**.
- Use reading commands to go through explanations on how to contact specific offices or get support.

This contact information is clearly provided on the university's site in the "Contact us" section, enabling easy access to support and enquiries.[2][4]
