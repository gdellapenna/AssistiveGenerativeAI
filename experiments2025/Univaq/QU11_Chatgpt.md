On the UNIVAQ home page ([https://www.univaq.it](https://www.univaq.it)), there is **no immediately visible “search for a person” or “directory” field** on the main content. However, there are ways to locate people (staff, professors, or departments) using the menus and subpages. Here’s how:

---

## 🔎 Visual navigation (sighted user)

1. Open the home page: `https://www.univaq.it`.
2. In the main menu, locate **“Ateneo”** — this is the University-wide section.
3. Hover over or click **“Ateneo”**, and look for submenu options such as:

   * **Personale / Staff** (staff directory)
   * **Dipartimenti** → choose a department → then see a list of professors, researchers, and administrative staff.
4. Some departments have their own **“Cerca docente / Cerca personale”** (search for teacher or staff) tools. You can click through the department and use their internal search form.
5. Alternatively, use the general **site search** (often in the top right corner — a magnifying glass icon) and type the person’s name.

---

## 🔉 Screen‑reader friendly steps

1. Open `https://www.univaq.it`. Wait until your screen reader announces the page title and first heading.
2. Use the **link list** or **tab through the main menu** to find **“Ateneo”**. Press Enter.
3. On the “Ateneo” page, navigate links or headings to locate:

   * **Personale / Staff**, **Faculty**, or **Dipartimenti**.
4. If you reach a department page, use headings/links to find staff lists or search boxes. Some departments have a form labeled **“Cerca docente / Cerca personale”** — type the person’s name and submit.
5. Alternatively, look for a **site-wide search field** (magnifying glass icon, often near the top of the page). Focus it, type the person’s name, and press Enter to see results.
