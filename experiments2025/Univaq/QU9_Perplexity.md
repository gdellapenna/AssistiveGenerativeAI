To open the secretary section on the University of L'Aquila website, you should look for and click on the menu item or link labeled "Segreteria virtuale" or "Segreteria studenti," which translates to "Virtual Secretary" or "Student Secretary."

Specifically:
- Usually, on the homepage or in the main navigation menus, there is a link to "Segreteria studenti" or "Segreteria virtuale."
- Alternatively, in quick access or footer links, look for the "Segreteria virtuale" section.
- This section leads to an online platform where students can manage administrative tasks like enrollment, exam registration, payment of fees, and more.
- The direct URL for the Segreteria virtuale is https://segreteriavirtuale.univaq.it, where students log in using their credentials or SPID digital identity.

Screen reader usage:
- Use Tab to navigate to the main menu and find "Segreteria" or "Segreteria virtuale."
- Press Enter to open the section.
- On the Segreteria virtuale page, use Tab and arrow keys to navigate through available student services and links.
- Alternatively, if you know the URL, you can enter it directly to go straight to the secretary services.

Thus, the "Segreteria virtuale" is the go-to area for student administrative services on the university site.[1][2]
