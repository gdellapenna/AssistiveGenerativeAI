On the **current homepage of Università degli Studi dell’Aquila**, you can switch the page to English using the **language selector** in the top utility bar.  

---

## 🖥️ Visual navigation (mouse/trackpad)

1. Look at the **very top of the page**, above the university logo.  
2. On the left side of the utility bar, you’ll see a small link labeled **“EN”**.  
3. Click on **“EN”**.  
   - This will reload the page in English, showing menus and content translated.  

---

## 🎧 Screen reader navigation (keyboard)

1. Navigate to the **top of the page** using the Tab key or by moving through links.  
2. The screen reader will announce “EN link.”  
3. Press **Enter** on “EN.”  
   - The page will reload in English.  

---

✅ So the exact path is: **Top utility bar → EN link**.
