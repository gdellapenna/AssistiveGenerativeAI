Yes, the University of L'Aquila offers a computer science degree. There are undergraduate and master's level programs in Computer Science available there. The Department of Information Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics at the university provides these courses, which include comprehensive training in computer science topics. The Master's program in particular is full-time and lasts 24 months, with modules covering applied mathematics, numerical modeling, and other advanced topics in the field. Admission is generally open with assessment of competencies, and the courses are held on the L'Aquila campus.

If you want to find the Computer Science program on the university website https://www.univaq.it, you would typically navigate to the "Departments" section, then to "Information Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics," where detailed course information is provided.

To navigate this with a screen reader from the homepage:
- Tab through the top navigation menu to "Departments" and press Enter.
- Navigate through the list of departments until you hear "Information Engineering, Computer Science and Mathematics" and select it.
- Inside this department page, use heading navigation or tab through links to locate detailed Computer Science degree and course information.

This confirms that the university offers computer science degrees, both at bachelor's and master's levels, with structured programs and relevant department support on campus.[1][3][6][7]
