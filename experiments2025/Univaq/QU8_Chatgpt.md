## 📢 Screen-reader friendly steps to reach the course catalogue

If you use a screen reader (e.g. NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver), here’s how to do it — using link/heading navigation:

1. Open `https://www.univaq.it` in your browser and wait for the page to load.

5. When you hear that link, press Enter. That will load the course-catalogue page, where you can explore the list of available degrees/programmes.
