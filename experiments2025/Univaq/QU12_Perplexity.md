On the University of L'Aquila website, you can switch the page language by using the language toggle option, usually labeled as "IT / EN" for Italian and English. This toggle is typically located near the top right corner of the page, often in the header area.

To switch languages:
- Visibly, look for a small button or link showing "IT" or "EN" at the top right.
- Click on "EN" to switch the website content to English or on "IT" to switch back to Italian.

For screen reader users:
- After the page loads, press **Tab** repeatedly until the screen reader announces a link or button labeled with a language code such as "Italian" or "English."
- Press **Enter** on the currently displayed language to activate the toggle and switch to the other language version.
- The screen reader will then start reading the content in the selected language.

This language switcher allows easy toggling of the website’s interface and content between Italian and English, supporting bilingual navigation and access.[4][11]
