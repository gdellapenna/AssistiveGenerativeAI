On the University of L'Aquila website, you can search for someone by using the dedicated staff directory feature. Here's how:

1. Look for a page or menu item called "Directory" or "Staff Directory" (English), or "Elenco" or "Personale" (Italian).
2. The directory page has a simple search box where you can enter a person's surname to find their position, department, address, phone number, fax, email, and website if available.
3. There is also an advanced search option that lets you filter by academic staff, technical and administrative staff, or administrative units.

With a screen reader:
- Use Tab to navigate to the main menu or site map to find the "Directory" or "Elenco" link and activate it with Enter.
- When on the directory page, locate the search input by tabbing and type the surname of the person you want to find.
- Tab to the search or submit button and activate it to perform the search.
- Results will be announced or displayed as links or text with contact information.

This feature allows you to find detailed contact and role information for university staff members effectively.[1][2]
