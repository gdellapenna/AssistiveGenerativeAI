## 2. Scegli il tuo profilo (Profile Selection Menu)

This menu allows users to filter the site content by their specific role.

**Visual Directions:** This is a set of distinct links or tabs labeled **"Scegli il tuo profilo"** (Choose your profile).

**Screen Reader Directions:** Look for the section or heading labeled **"Scegli il tuo profilo"**. The menu options are:

* **Futura matricola** (Future student/applicant)
* **Studente** (Current student)
* **Studente internazionale** (International student)
* **Laureata e laureato** (Graduate)
* **Personale** (Staff)
* **Imprese** (Companies/Businesses)

## 3. Primary Site Menu

This is the main horizontal navigation bar that organizes the core university functions.

**Visual Directions:** These are the largest, main links located just below the profile selection.

**Screen Reader Directions:** Look for the main **Navigation** element or the top-level list of links. The options are:

### 1. Ateneo (University/Institution)

* This section relates to the **history, organization, and administration** of the university.

### 2. Didattica (Education/Teaching)

* This section relates to **courses, academic calendar, student services, and enrollment**.

### 3. Ricerca (Research)

* This section relates to **research structures, funding, and technology transfer**.

### 4. Internazionalità (International)

* This section relates to **international programs, student mobility (like Erasmus), and international course catalogue**.
