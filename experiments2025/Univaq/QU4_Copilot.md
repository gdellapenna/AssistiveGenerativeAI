- **Mobilità e Erasmus** (Mobility and Erasmus programs)  
- **Segreterie studenti** (Student offices)  
- **Regolamenti didattici** (Teaching regulations)  
- **Didattica a distanza** (Distance learning)

3. The submenu items will be read out in sequence:  
   - “Offerta formativa link”  
   - “Calendario didattico link”  
   - “Orari delle lezioni link”  
   - “Esami link”  
   - “Tirocini e stage link”  
   - “Mobilità e Erasmus link”  
   - “Segreterie studenti link”  
   - “Regolamenti didattici link”  
   - “Didattica a distanza link”  
4. Use the **Tab key** or arrow keys to move through these options, and press **Enter** to activate the one you want.
