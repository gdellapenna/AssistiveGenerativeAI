Great question — the **course catalogue** is part of the “Didattica” menu on the Università degli Studi dell’Aquila homepage. Here’s how to reach it:

---

## 🖥️ Visual navigation (with a mouse or trackpad)

1. **Locate the main navigation bar**:  
   - It’s just below the university logo, spanning horizontally across the page.  
   - You’ll see items like *Ateneo*, *Didattica*, *Ricerca*, *Internazionale*, etc.

2. **Hover over “Didattica”**:  
   - When you place your mouse pointer over **“Didattica”**, a dropdown menu will appear.  

3. **Find “Offerta formativa”**:  
   - In the dropdown, click on **“Offerta formativa”** (Educational offer).  
   - This is the entry point to the **course catalogue**, where degree programs are listed.

---

## 🎧 Screen reader navigation (keyboard interaction)

1. **Navigate to the main menu**:  
   - Use the **Tab key** or navigate by links until the screen reader announces “Didattica link.”  

2. **Expand the submenu**:  
   - Press **Enter** on “Didattica.”  
   - The submenu items will then be available.  

3. **Select “Offerta formativa”**:  
   - Use the arrow keys or Tab to move through submenu items.  
   - The screen reader will announce “Offerta formativa link.”  
   - Press **Enter** to activate it.  

4. **Result**:  
   - The page will load the **course catalogue**, listing available degree programs (including Informatica / Computer Science if offered).

---

✅ So the exact path is: **Main navigation bar → Didattica → Offerta formativa**.
