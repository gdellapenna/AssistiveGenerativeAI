On the **current homepage of Università degli Studi dell’Aquila**, the secretary section is accessible through the **Didattica menu**.  

---

## 🖥️ Visual navigation (mouse/trackpad)

1. Look at the **main navigation bar** just below the university logo.  
2. Hover your mouse over **“Didattica”**.  
3. A dropdown menu will appear.  
4. In that dropdown, click on **“Segreterie studenti”** (Student secretaries).  
   - This link opens the section dedicated to student secretariat services (enrollment, exams, documents, etc.).

---

## 🎧 Screen reader navigation (keyboard)

1. Use the **Tab key** or navigate by links until the screen reader announces “Didattica link.”  
2. Press **Enter** to expand the submenu.  
3. Move through the submenu items with arrow keys or Tab.  
4. The screen reader will announce “Segreterie studenti link.”  
5. Press **Enter** to activate it.  
   - This will load the student secretary section page.

---

✅ So the exact path is: **Main navigation bar → Didattica → Segreterie studenti**.
