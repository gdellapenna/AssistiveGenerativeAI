To reach the university course catalogue, you need to use the **Primary Site Menu** and navigate through the **"Didattica"** (Education/Teaching) section.

You will need to perform two steps: selecting the main category and then selecting the specific catalogue link from the submenu.

### Step 1: Select the "Didattica" (Education/Teaching) Main Menu Item

**Visual Directions:**
Locate the main horizontal menu below the profile selection area. Click on the main link labeled **"Didattica"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the main navigation area. Find and activate the link labeled **"Didattica"**.

### Step 2: Select the "Course catalogue" Link from the Submenu

Once the "Didattica" section is activated (which may open a dropdown or lead to a new section on the page), you must find the specific link for the courses.

**Visual Directions:**
In the list of submenu options that appear under "Didattica," look for and click the link labeled **"Course catalogue"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
After activating the "Didattica" link, the submenu items will become available. Use your navigation commands to read through the list of links until you find the one explicitly labeled **"Course catalogue"**. Activate this link.

***
*Note: The "Didattica" submenu contains two similar links: "Offerta formativa" (Educational Offering, in Italian) and "Course catalogue" (in English). Selecting "Course catalogue" is the most direct way for an English-speaking user to reach the list of programs.*
