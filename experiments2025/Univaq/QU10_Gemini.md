The process of enrollment (immatricolazione) is governed by rules and deadlines that are accessible through the page structure, primarily under the **"Didattica"** (Education) or the **"Scegli il tuo profilo"** (Choose your profile) menus.

Since the full enrollment process involves multiple steps on pages not currently visible, I will guide you to the link that will take you to the relevant information page.

### 1. Using the "Future Student" Profile

The most direct way to find enrollment information is by selecting the profile designated for new applicants.

**Visual Directions:**
Locate the section labeled **"Scegli il tuo profilo"** (Choose your profile). Click on the first link in this list, which is **"Futura matricola"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the section labeled **"Scegli il tuo profilo"**. Find and activate the link labeled **"Futura matricola"** (Future student/applicant).

### 2. Using the Student Services Menu

You can also find detailed procedural information under the general student services menu, which is located in the deeper site structure (this acts as a secondary menu, though it is fully detailed on the current home page).

**Visual Directions (in the secondary/expanded menu):**
Find the comprehensive menu section labeled **"Studenti"** (Students). Look for the link within this list that mentions the full enrollment cycle.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the large menu section labeled **"Studenti"**. Find and activate the link labeled:
* **Immatricolarsi - frequentare - concludere** (Enroll - attend - graduate)

### 3. Reviewing Deadlines

While this doesn't start the enrollment process, it provides the key dates for it.

**Visual Directions:**
Scroll down to the **"Scadenze"** (Deadlines) section. The first item relates to enrollment.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Look for the section labeled **"Scadenze"**. Note the dates for:
* **luglio - ottobre: Ammissioni e immatricolazioni** (Admissions and enrollment)

Activating the links in **Step 1** or **Step 2** will take you to the page that describes the precise documents and steps needed for enrollment.
