The "Didattica" item in the main menu of the University of L'Aquila website leads to a submenu (or a page with navigation links) focused on teaching and academic offerings. The submenu options or navigational subsections under "Didattica" include:

- Course catalogue (Catalogo corsi): A web application to consult the university's full educational offer by year of enrollment. Here you can find detailed information about study programs, course objectives, syllabuses, and more.
- Normativa (Regulations): Documents and rules governing academic activities.
- Documenti di programmazione (Planning documents): Relevant academic planning documents.
- CCRUA (Commissione paritetica per la valutazione della didattica): Committee for teaching evaluation.
- Sistema di Assicurazione della Qualità (Quality Assurance System): Information on the quality assurance process.
- Ranking internazionali (International rankings): University rankings.
- Sviluppo sostenibile (Sustainable development).
- UnivAQ Provider ECM: Continuing medical education provider.
- Centri di servizio di Ateneo (University service centers).
- Offerta formativa (Educational offer).
- Offerta formativa per la Pubblica amministrazione (Educational offer for public administration).
- Innovazione didattica (Teaching innovation).
- Calendario accademico e principali scadenze amministrative (Academic calendar and main administrative deadlines).
- Tirocini e stages per studenti e laureati (Internships and traineeships for students and graduates).
- Erasmus italiano (Italian Erasmus program).
- Diploma supplement.
- Esami di Stato (State exams).
- Servizi per studentesse e studenti con disabilità e con DSA (Services for students with disabilities and learning disorders).
- Valutazione della didattica (Teaching evaluation).
- Modulistica (Forms).
- Link utili (Useful links).
- Strutture di ricerca (Research facilities).
- Formazione alla ricerca (Research training).

This submenu is usually found on the left side as a navigation menu or as a list on the "Didattica" page.

***

How to access this submenu using a screen reader:
- From the homepage or any page, press **Tab** until the screen reader announces "Didattica" in the main menu, then press **Enter**.
- On the "Didattica" page, use the Tab key or heading navigation commands to move through the list of submenu links listed above.
- Each submenu item is a link that you can activate with **Enter** to access detailed content.
- Use arrow keys or additional Tab presses to move between submenu links if they are arranged in a side menu or vertical list.

This detailed "Didattica" submenu provides comprehensive access to all academic and teaching-related services, information, and resources on this university's website.[1][3][4]
