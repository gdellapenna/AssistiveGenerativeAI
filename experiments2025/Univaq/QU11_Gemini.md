You can search for someone at the university using the dedicated **Search Box** or by navigating to the **Directory/Who and Where** link.

### 1. Using the Search Box

The search box is the most general way to find information, which includes searching for names.

**Visual Directions:**
Look near the top right of the page, above the main navigation. You will find an input field labeled **"cerca"** (search).

**Screen Reader Directions:**
1.  Navigate to the **Search Form** or the **edit field** labeled **"cerca"**.
2.  Type the name of the person you are looking for into this field.
3.  Activate the adjacent **button** (usually a search icon) to submit your query.

### 2. Using the Staff Directory Link

This link is specifically designed to take you to the university's directory of personnel and offices.

**Visual Directions:**
Look in the **"Accesso veloce"** (Quick Access) menu near the top of the page. Click on the link labeled **"Chi e dove"**.

**Screen Reader Directions:**
Navigate to the list of links under **"Accesso veloce"**. Find and activate the link labeled **"Chi e dove"** (Who and where / Directory). This will take you to a dedicated search page for personnel.
