The Cambridge English Dictionary page has menus that help users navigate various sections of the site. Here are the main menu options typically available on the page:

### Main Menu Options:
- **English Dictionary**: The default section where you can search and look up English words and their definitions.
- **Thesaurus**: A linked section where you can find synonyms and related words.
- **Word Lists**: Access to curated lists of vocabulary for different levels or categories.
- **Grammar**: A section offering grammar guides and reference materials.
- **Practice**: Exercises and activities to practice English vocabulary and grammar.
- **Games & Quizzes**: Language games and quizzes for learning in a fun way.
- **Cambridge English Exams**: Information and resources related to Cambridge exams.
- **Blog**: Articles and tips related to English language learning.
- **Support or Help**: A help section or contact for assistance with the dictionary or website usage.

### Location and Navigation:
- The menu is usually presented as a horizontal navigation bar near the top of the page, just below or beside the search bar.
- Each menu option is a clickable link labeled clearly with the section name.
- Some menus may have dropdowns or submenus for more specific topics or resources.
- For screen reader users, the menu is organized as a list of navigation links, accessible by using standard landmark or list navigation commands.
- The menu is consistent across pages for easy orientation.

This menu structure allows users to quickly jump to desired resources beyond just looking up single words, supporting both learning and exploration in English.[5][7][11]
