Here’s how you can do it on the Cambridge English Dictionary homepage:

## 👀 Visual Instructions
1. Scroll down past the **Popular Searches** section.  
2. You’ll see a horizontal strip labeled **“Browse the English Dictionary”**.  
3. This strip contains clickable links for numbers (0–9) and letters (A–Z).  
4. Find the **letter “A”** at the very beginning of this strip.  
5. Click directly on the **“A” link**.  
   - This will open a page listing all dictionary entries that begin with the letter “A”.

---

## 🎧 Screen Reader Instructions
1. Use **heading navigation** until you hear *“Browse the English Dictionary”*.  
2. After that heading, move forward with **Tab** or arrow keys.  
3. The screen reader will announce each letter as a **link**: *“Link: A”*, *“Link: B”*, etc.  
4. When you hear *“Link: A”*, press **Enter**.  
   - This action loads the list of words starting with “A”.
