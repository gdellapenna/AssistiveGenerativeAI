Here’s how you can search for a specific word on the Cambridge English Dictionary homepage:

---

## 🖥️ Visual Directions (sighted navigation)

1. At the very **top center of the page**, you’ll see a large **white search box** with a magnifying glass icon on the right.
2. Click inside the search box.  
   - The cursor will appear, ready for typing.
3. Type the word you want to look up (for example, *apple*).
4. Press the **Enter key** on your keyboard, or click the **magnifying glass icon** to the right of the box.
5. The page will reload and show you the definition of the word you searched.

---

## 🎧 Screen Reader Directions

1. From the top of the page, press **H** until you hear the heading **“English Dictionary.”**
2. After that heading, press **Tab** once.  
   - Your screen reader will announce: “Edit box, search.”
3. Type the word you want to look up (e.g., *apple*).
4. Press **Enter**.  
   - The page will load the definition entry for that word.

---

✅ In short: the **search box at the very top** is the main tool for finding a specific word. You just type the word and press Enter.
