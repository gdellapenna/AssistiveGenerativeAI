## 🎧 Screen Reader–Friendly Structure

- **Landmarks:**
  - **Banner / Header:** Contains logo, search form, and navigation links.
  - **Main:** Holds dictionary content (search, browse, word of the day, new words, blog).
  - **Footer:** Contains informational and policy links.

- **Headings:**
  - “English Dictionary” → main heading near the search box.
  - “Browse the English Dictionary” → heading for alphabet links.
  - “Word of the Day” → heading for featured word.
  - “New Words” → heading for recent additions.
  - “Blog” → heading for articles.

- **Interactive Elements:**
  - **Search box:** Announced as “Edit box, search.”
  - **Popular searches:** Links announced as “link, through,” “link, present,” etc.
  - **Alphabet links:** Announced as “link, a,” “link, b,” etc.
  - **Word of the Day:** Announced as “link, jamboree.”
  - **New Words:** Links announced with word names.
  - **Blog articles:** Links announced with article titles.

- **Navigation Tips:**
  - Use **H** to jump between headings (NVDA/JAWS).
  - Use **Tab** to move through interactive elements.
  - Use **Insert+F7 (JAWS/NVDA)** to open the link list and quickly jump to any word or article.
