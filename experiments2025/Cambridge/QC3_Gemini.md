### Finding words beginning with "A"

**👁️ Visual Directions:**

1.  Scroll down or look below the main search area for a section titled **Browse the English Dictionary**.
2.  In this section, you will see a series of links laid out horizontally: `0–9 a b c d e f g h i j k l m n o p q r s t u v w x y z`.
3.  Click on the letter **a** link. This will take you to a new page listing all words starting with that letter.

**🔊 Screen Reader Directions:**

1.  Use the heading navigation command (e.g., pressing `H` or `3` if it's an H3) to find the heading: **Browse the English Dictionary**.
2.  Immediately following this heading, look for a group of **Links**.
3.  Navigate through the links (e.g., using `Tab` or the link navigation command) until you find the link labeled simply **a**.
4.  Activate this link (e.g., by pressing `Enter` or `Spacebar`).
