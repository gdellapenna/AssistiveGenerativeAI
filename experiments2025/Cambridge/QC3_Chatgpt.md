Here’s how to locate and open the list of words beginning with **A** on the Cambridge English Dictionary homepage:

---

## 🖥️ Visual Directions (sighted navigation)

1. Scroll down a little from the main search bar until you see the section titled **“Browse the English Dictionary.”**
2. In this section, you’ll see a row of clickable letters: **A, B, C, … Z, 0–9.**
3. The very first link is **“A.”**
4. Click directly on the **“A” link**.  
   - This will open a page listing all dictionary entries that begin with the letter A.

---

## 🎧 Screen Reader Directions

1. From the top of the page, press **H** until you reach the heading **“Browse the English Dictionary.”**
2. After landing on this heading, press **Tab** or use the **Link List (Insert+F7 in JAWS/NVDA)**.
3. You’ll hear links announced as “link, a,” “link, b,” “link, c,” etc.
4. Move to the first link: **“a.”**
5. Press **Enter** to activate the link.  
   - This will load the page showing all words beginning with the letter A.

---

✅ In short: the **“A” link** is the very first option under the **“Browse the English Dictionary”** heading.
