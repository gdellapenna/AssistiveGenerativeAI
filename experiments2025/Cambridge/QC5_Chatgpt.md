Here’s how to find the **search bar** on the Cambridge English Dictionary homepage:

---

## 🖥️ Visual Directions (sighted navigation)

1. Look at the very **top center of the page**.  
   - Just below the Cambridge Dictionary logo, you’ll see a large **white rectangular box**.  
   - On its right side, there’s a small **magnifying glass icon**.
2. That box is the **search bar**.  
   - Click inside it to place your cursor.  
   - You can now type the word you want to look up.

---

## 🎧 Screen Reader Directions

1. From the top of the page, press **H** until you hear the heading **“English Dictionary.”**
2. After that heading, press **Tab** once.  
   - Your screen reader will announce: **“Edit box, search.”**
3. That is the **search bar**.  
   - Type your word directly into this edit box.  
   - Press **Enter** to search.

---

✅ In short: the search bar is the **large white box at the very top center of the page**, and with a screen reader it’s announced as **“Edit box, search”** right after the “English Dictionary” heading.
