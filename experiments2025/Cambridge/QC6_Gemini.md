Yes, there are several menus on this page, structured primarily in the header and the footer.

### Describing the Menu Options

#### 1. Header Menu (Primary Navigation)

This menu is located at the top of the page and offers links to the main sections of the Cambridge Dictionary website.

| **Menu Name** | **Options** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Main Navigation** | **Dictionary**, **Translate**, **Grammar**, **Thesaurus**, **+Plus**, **Games** |
| **User Utilities** | **Log in / Sign up**, **English (UK)** (for changing language) |

**👁️ Visual Directions:**

* Look for the horizontal row of links near the top of the page, to the right of the Cambridge Dictionary logo.

**🔊 Screen Reader Directions:**

* Navigate to the **Header landmark**. The links are readily available here. You can use the link navigation command to cycle through options like **Dictionary**, **Translate**, and **Log in**.

---

#### 2. Footer Menus (Secondary Navigation)

These menus are located at the very bottom of the page and are grouped under specific headings, offering links to information about the site and its services.

| **Menu Heading** | **Options** |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Learn** | Learn, New Words, Help, In Print, Word of the Year 2021, Word of the Year 2022, Word of the Year 2023, Word of the Year 2024 |
| **Develop** | Develop, Dictionary API, Double-Click Lookup, Search Widgets, License Data |
| **About** | About, **Accessibility**, Cambridge English, Cambridge University Press & Assessment, Cookies Settings, Cookies and Privacy, Corpus, Terms of Use |

**👁️ Visual Directions:**

* Scroll all the way to the bottom of the page. You will see three columns of links organized under the headings: **Learn**, **Develop**, and **About**.

**🔊 Screen Reader Directions:**

* Navigate to the **Footer landmark**. You can use the heading navigation command to jump to the **Learn**, **Develop**, and **About** headings, and then use the link navigation command to explore the options under each heading, such as **Accessibility** or **Help**.
