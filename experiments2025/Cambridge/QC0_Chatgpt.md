### Getting started

I’m ready to guide you step by step on a single web page, both visually and with screen reader–friendly instructions. To do that accurately, I need the exact URL of the page.

- **What I need:** The URL of the page you want to use.
- **Optional but helpful:** Tell me which screen reader you use (NVDA, JAWS, VoiceOver) and whether you navigate mainly by keyboard.

### How I will guide you

- **Visual directions:** I’ll describe the page layout, name the specific elements to click, and explain where they are on the page.
- **Screen reader directions:** I’ll give keyboard and screen reader commands (e.g., heading navigation, landmarks, forms mode), element names/roles, and the order to activate them.

Share the URL when you’re ready, and I’ll walk you through the exact steps on that page.
