Here’s how to find the **search bar** on the Cambridge English Dictionary homepage:

---

## 👀 Visual Instructions
1. Go to the **very top of the page** (the header area).  
2. In the center of the header, you’ll see a **rectangular search box**.  
   - It has faint placeholder text like *“Search English”*.  
   - To the right of the box, there’s usually a **magnifying glass icon**.  
3. Click inside the box to place your cursor there.  
4. You can now type the word you want to look up.

---

## 🎧 Screen Reader Instructions
1. From the top of the page, use **Tab** or the quick navigation shortcut **“/”** (on many browsers) until your screen reader announces:  
   - *“Search English, edit text.”*  
2. This is the **search bar**.  
3. Type the word you want.  
4. Press **Enter** to submit your search.  
   - The results page will load with the definition of the word.  

---

✅ So: the search bar is located **at the very top center of the page header**, and your screen reader will announce it as an **edit text field labeled “Search English.”**
