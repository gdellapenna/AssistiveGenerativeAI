Here’s how you can access **subscription information** on The Wall Street Journal (WSJ.com), both visually and via a screen reader.

---

## 🖥 Visual directions (sighted view)

1. Open the WSJ homepage.
2. Look toward the **top-right corner** of the page. You will typically see buttons or links labeled:

   * “Subscribe”
   * “Sign In” or “My Journal”
3. Click on **“Subscribe”**. This will open a page detailing subscription options: digital-only, print + digital, pricing plans, special offers, etc.
4. On the subscription page, you can usually:

   * Compare subscription types (Digital, Premium, All Access, Print + Digital)
   * Enter personal information to create an account or sign in
   * Choose payment method and start subscription

---

## 🎧 Screen‑reader / keyboard directions

1. When the page loads, use your screen reader to **navigate by links** (or “next link”) until you hear a link labeled **“Subscribe”**. This is usually near the top of the page, before the main news content.
2. Press **Enter** on the “Subscribe” link to open the subscription page.
3. On the subscription page, navigate through headings or links: you will hear sections like:

   * “Digital Subscription”
   * “Print + Digital”
   * “Pricing / Offers”
4. Use your screen reader commands to explore each section. You can then follow prompts to:

   * Compare subscription types
   * Select a plan
   * Begin sign-up / checkout process

---

⚠️ **Tip:** Even if you don’t intend to subscribe, simply visiting the subscription page allows you to hear all available plans, pricing, and benefits without requiring you to proceed further.
