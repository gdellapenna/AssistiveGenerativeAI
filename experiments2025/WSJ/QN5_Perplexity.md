## Visual Directions to Search
Locate the search icon (magnifying glass) in the top-right header area, next to login/subscribe links. Click it to expand a search bar; type your keywords (e.g., "Trump tariffs") and press Enter or click the search button. Results appear in a dedicated page with filters for dates, sections, and advanced options like subjects or regions.[1][2]

## Screen Reader Directions
- Upon page load, use screen reader heading navigation (H key in NVDA/JAWS) to reach the top navigation landmark.
- Tab through interactive elements until announcing "Search icon" or "Search button" (often ARIA-labeled "Search WSJ.com").
- Activate (Enter/Space) to open the search field; type keywords, then Tab to "Search" button and activate.
- Results list as headings/links; use R key (regions) or L key (lists) to navigate filtered results.[2][1]

## Advanced Search Tips
Access "Advanced Search" via a link below or beside the search bar for phrases, company names, date ranges, or sections. If logged in as subscriber, full articles load; otherwise, previews show with subscribe prompts.[2]
