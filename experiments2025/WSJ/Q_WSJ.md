Your answers must refer *only to the specified web page*: *do not report any information that is not deducible from the page contents and HTML structure*, and *do not refer to any other page in your answers*. Then I will present you with a series of requests, and you will explain to me how to fulfill them on that page - by analyzing its content, interactive elements, and navigation structures - detailing which elements to interact with, where they are located, and how to use them.
