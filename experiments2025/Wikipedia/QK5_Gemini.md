You can switch the language of the Main Page by navigating to the section listing **Wikipedia languages** at the bottom of the page.

### **How to Switch Languages**

#### **Visual Directions (General User)**

1.  Scroll all the way down to the very bottom of the page.
2.  Look for the major section heading titled "**Wikipedia languages**."
3.  Underneath this heading, you will see lists of languages. They are grouped by the size of their Wikipedia edition (e.g., "1,000,000+ articles", "250,000+ articles", etc.).
4.  Find the language you want (for example, "Español" for Spanish or "Français" for French) and click on the link next to it.

#### **Screen Reader Directions (Blind User)**

1.  **Use the "Next Heading" command** (often **H**) to navigate to the major sections.
2.  Keep pressing the command until you hear the heading: "**Wikipedia languages**."
3.  **Use the "Next Link" command** (often **Tab** or **L**) to move through the languages listed in the section.
4.  The links are usually presented with the language name. For example, in the "1,000,000+ articles" list, you can find links for:
    * **العربية** (Arabic)
    * **Deutsch** (German)
    * **Español** (Spanish)
    * **Français** (French)
    * **Italiano** (Italian)
    * ...and many more.

5.  When you find the link for your desired language, press **Enter** or the appropriate activation key to go to the Main Page in that language.
