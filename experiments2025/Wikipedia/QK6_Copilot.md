Yes, you can export the contents of the Wikipedia Main Page to a PDF using the built-in tools on the page. Here's how:

---

## 🖥️ Visual Instructions

### Step-by-step:
1. **Look at the left-hand sidebar** of the page.
2. Scroll down to the section labeled **“Print/export.”**
3. Click on the link **“Download as PDF.”**
4. You’ll be taken to a new page where the PDF will be generated.
5. Once ready, click the **“Download”** button to save the file to your device.

---

## 🗣️ Screen Reader Instructions

### Step-by-step:
1. Press `H` until you reach the heading **“Print/export.”**
2. Arrow down to the link labeled **“Download as PDF.”**
3. Press `Enter` to activate the link.
4. Wait for the new page to load — your screen reader will announce the content.
5. Use Tab to navigate to the **“Download”** button and press `Enter` to save the PDF.
