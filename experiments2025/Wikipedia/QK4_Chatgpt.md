### How to navigate the menu visually

* A sighted user will see a vertical sidebar on the left side of the screen (under the logo) containing these menu links.
* Locate the left sidebar, then scan down until you find “Main menu” heading, and then the list of links (Main page, Contents, etc.).
* Then a little further down, you will find the “Contribute” heading and its links.
* A search box appears near the top of the page (usually right-of or above the main menu area).
* Near the top right (or somewhere prominent) you will find links such as “Donate”, “Create account”, “Log in”.
* If you are signed in, “Personal tools” appear (top right) with things like your username, preferences, etc.

### How to navigate the menu with a screen reader

* Use the **Landmark** or **Region** navigation to jump to the “Navigation” region (often labelled “Main menu” or “Sidebar navigation”).

* Inside that region:

  * Listen for a heading labelled *“Main menu”*. Under that heading, you’ll hear the links in the order: *“Main page link”*, *“Contents link”*, *“Current events link”*, *“Random article link”*, *“About Wikipedia link”*, *“Contact us link”*.
  * After those, you’ll hear another heading labelled *“Contribute”*. Under that, links: *“Help link”*, *“Learn to edit link”*, *“Community portal link”*, *“Recent changes link”*, *“Upload file link”*, *“Special pages link”*.
  * Then you’ll hear a heading (or link) for *“Search”*, where you can find the search field. You may have to tab into the search edit box (you’ll hear something like “Search edit”). Type your query and press **Enter**.
  * Then find a heading or link labelled *“Appearance”*, under which you’ll hear *“Donate link”*, *“Create account link”*, *“Log in link”*.
  * If you are signed in, a region labelled *“Personal tools”* appears (top right) with links like *“Preferences link”*, *“Watchlist link”*, etc.

* Keyboard steps:

  * Press **F6** / **Ctrl+F6** (depending on screen reader) or **Ctrl+M** (NVDA) to move between regions/landmarks until you reach the sidebar navigation.
  * Use **H** (heading navigation) until you hit the “Main menu” heading.
  * Once inside the menu, use **Tab** or **K** (links navigation) to move through each link.
  * To jump to the search box, you could press **Tab** until you hear “Search edit”. Then type your query and press **Enter**.
  * To jump to “Donate” or “Log in”, you can use **H** again until you hear the “Appearance” heading then use **Tab** to move to the links.
