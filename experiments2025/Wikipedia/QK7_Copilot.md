To search for a specific entry on the Wikipedia Main Page, you’ll use the search box located at the top-right corner of the page. Here’s how to do it:

---

## 🖥️ Visual Instructions

### Step-by-step:
1. **Locate the search box** in the top-right corner of the page.
2. Click inside the box labeled **“Search Wikipedia.”**
3. Type the name of the topic or entry you want to find (e.g., “Leonardo da Vinci”).
4. Press **Enter** on your keyboard.
5. You’ll be taken directly to the article if it exists, or to a search results page if multiple matches are found.

---

## 🗣️ Screen Reader Instructions

### Step-by-step:
1. Press the `E` key to jump to the **search edit field**.
   - Your screen reader will announce: “Search Wikipedia edit.”
2. Type the name of the topic you’re looking for.
3. Press **Enter**.
4. If the article exists, it will open directly.
   - Otherwise, you’ll land on a search results page with links to matching entries.
5. Use heading navigation (`H`) or Tab to explore the results.
   - Press `Enter` on the link that matches your intended topic.
