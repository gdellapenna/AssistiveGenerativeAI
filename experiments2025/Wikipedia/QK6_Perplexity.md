You can export the contents of the Wikipedia main page to a PDF file using Wikipedia's built-in feature. Here is how to do it step by step:

Visually:
1. Near the left side of the page, locate the sidebar menu.
2. Scroll down to the section titled "Print/export."
3. Click the link labeled "Download as PDF."
4. Wikipedia will start rendering the page into a PDF. After some moments, a new page will appear with a download button.
5. Click the "Download" button to save the PDF file to your device.

For screen reader users:
1. Use heading navigation or landmark navigation to find the "Print/export" section in the left sidebar. It is a heading grouping a small list of links.
2. Navigate to the link named "Download as PDF" in that section.
3. Activate the link by pressing Enter or your screen reader's activate command.
4. Wait while Wikipedia prepares the PDF; your screen reader will announce when the page changes.
5. A new link labeled "Download" will appear. Navigate to it and activate it to save the PDF.

The generated PDF includes the entire visible content of the current Wikipedia page, plus the page’s URL, last modified date, and licensing information. Hyperlinks within the article are preserved though not visually highlighted.

If you prefer, an alternative is to use your browser’s Print feature to save the page as a PDF, but the above Wikipedia tool is designed specifically for this purpose and generally creates a cleaner PDF.
