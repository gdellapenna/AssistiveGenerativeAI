### Information about Laws and Regulations

#### **Visual Directions (What you see):**

You can find information about laws and regulations in two main places:

1.  **Main Menu Link:** Look for the main menu option labeled **"Atti e normativa"** (Acts and Regulations). Clicking this will take you to a dedicated section for official documents, laws, and administrative acts.
2.  **Latest News:** One of the prominent news titles explicitly mentions a law:
    * **"MUR, 160 milioni di euro per gli Enti di Ricerca: la Camera approva definitivamente il Dl Università"** (MUR, 160 million euros for Research Bodies: the Chamber definitively approves the **University Decree-Law**). Clicking on this news title will provide the full article and context about this recently approved law.

#### **Screen Reader Directions (How your reader finds the elements):**

1.  **Accessing the Archive:**
    * **Action:** Navigate to the main menu (using the **Navigation Landmark** or the "Menu" button).
    * **Focus:** Look for the link labeled **"Atti e normativa"** (Acts and Regulations).
    * **Guidance:** Press $\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$ on this link to leave the homepage and go to the laws and regulations archive.

2.  **Accessing the Latest Law News:**
    * **Action:** Use your **Headings shortcut** ($\text{H}$) to navigate the news section in the main content area.
    * **Focus:** Look for the heading/link that contains the key terms: **"MUR, 160 milioni di euro per gli Enti di Ricerca: la Camera approva definitivamente il Dl Università"**.
    * **Guidance:** Press $\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$ on this link to read the details of the University Decree-Law.
