Yes, you can find information on competitions (Concorsi) from this page by navigating through the main menu structure.

### How to Access Competition Information

#### **Visual Directions (What you see):**

1.  **Open the Menu:** Click on the main **"Menu"** link/button located at the top of the page.
2.  **Select the Ministry Section:** Locate and click the main category link titled **"Ministero"** (Ministry).
3.  **Find the Competitions Sub-menu:** Within the sub-menu that opens for "Ministero," look for the link labeled **"Concorsi e Avvisi"** (Competitions and Notices).
4.  **Click to Proceed:** Click on **"Concorsi e Avvisi"** to go to the page containing the competition information.

#### **Screen Reader Directions (How your reader finds the elements):**

1.  **Navigate to the Menu:** Use the link or button shortcut to find the **"Menu"** element and activate it ($\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$).
2.  **Access the 'Ministero' Link:** Use your link shortcut ($\text{L}$) or $\text{TAB}$ key to locate the main link: **"Ministero, Open submenu"** (Ministry, Open submenu).
3.  **Expand the Submenu:** Activate the **"Ministero"** link/submenu ($\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$).
4.  **Locate 'Concorsi':** Navigate the newly expanded sub-links until you find the link labeled **"Concorsi e Avvisi"** (Competitions and Notices).
5.  **Proceed to the Page:** Activate the **"Concorsi e Avvisi"** link ($\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$) to load the dedicated page for competitions.
