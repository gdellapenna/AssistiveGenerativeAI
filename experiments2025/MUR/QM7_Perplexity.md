## Login Availability

No, the homepage at https://www.mur.gov.it/it does not contain a login form, username/password fields, or direct "Accesso riservato" button based on its visible content, which focuses on news, minister profile, and navigation to sections like Ministero and Attività.[8][11]

## Visual Navigation Check

Scan the header, main content, sidebar, and footer—no interactive login elements appear. Main menu items like "Ministero" lead to subpages (e.g., Concorsi e Avvisi), but the homepage itself shows only informational links such as "Vai alla sezione" for biography or multimedia.[11][12]

## Screen Reader Navigation Check

Press Insert+F7 for full links list—no entries like "Login," "Accesso," "Username," or "Password" fields. Sequential reading (down arrow) or H for headings covers news titles, "In Evidenza," and "IL MINISTRO" without login prompts. R for regions jumps to navigation/main/footer, confirming absence.[8][11]
