## Main Navigation Menu

Yes, the homepage includes a main navigation menu in the header area, typically as a horizontal top menu bar below the ministry logo/title. Options cover key sections of the site: Ministero (Ministry organization and structure), Il Ministro (The Minister), Attività (Activities), Dichiarazioni (Statements), Multimedia (Media content), and likely additional items like Aree Tematiche (Thematic Areas), Atti e Normativa (Acts and Regulations), and PN RR (National Recovery Plan).[4][7][11]

## Secondary Navigation Elements

- Footer or sidebar menus provide expanded navigation, including "Mappa del Sito" (Site Map) linking to full structure like Ministero subpages (Sedi e Strutture, Organizzazione).
- "In Evidenza" (Highlights) section acts as a menu-like list with links to featured topics such as university funding, research entities, and educational norms.
- Minister-specific submenu: "BIOGRAFIA" (Biography), "Leggi tutte le dichiarazioni del Ministro" (Read all Minister's statements), "Guarda la sezione Multimedia" (View Multimedia section).[2][11]

## Screen Reader Access to Menus

Use H key for headings to reach "Ministero" or "Il Ministro" menu anchors. Insert+F7 opens links list—filter or select items like "Vai alla sezione" under each menu option (e.g., for biography or activities). Landmark navigation (R key) jumps to "navigation" region for the main menu, then tab through or arrow to subitems; sequential reading announces menu labels before news content.[11][4]
