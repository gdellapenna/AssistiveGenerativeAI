Yes — the homepage of the Ministry of University and Research (https://www.mur.gov.it/it) does provide access to information about **competitions (concorsi)**, but it is not directly in the “In evidenza” news section. Instead, it is available through the **main navigation menu**.

---

## 🖥️ Visual directions (sighted navigation)

1. At the top of the page, locate the **horizontal menu bar** under the ministry’s logo.  
2. In this menu, hover over or click on **“Servizi”** (Services).  
3. Within “Servizi”, there is a subsection for **Concorsi** (Competitions).  
4. Click on “Concorsi” to open the dedicated page where the ministry publishes calls, competitions, and related information.  

---

## 🎧 Screen reader directions (step-by-step)

1. After the page loads, press `D` (NVDA) or `R` (JAWS) to jump to the **navigation landmark**.  
2. Use the **Tab key** or arrow keys to move through the menu items. Your screen reader will announce them in order: “Ministero, link … PNRR, link … Università, link … AFAM, link … Ricerca, link … Studenti, link … Normativa, link … Comunicazione, link … Servizi, link”.  
3. Stop at **“Servizi, link”** and press **Enter**.  
4. On the “Servizi” page, navigate by headings or links until you hear **“Concorsi”**.  
5. Activate the **“Concorsi” link** with Enter. This will open the section with information about competitions.  

---

✅ So yes, you can access competition information from the homepage by going through the **Servizi → Concorsi** path in the main menu.
