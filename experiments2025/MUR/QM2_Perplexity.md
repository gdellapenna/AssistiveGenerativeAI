## Screen Reader Navigation Guide

To explore structure with a screen reader (e.g., NVDA/JAWS/VoiceOver):
- Press H to jump headings: First H1/H2 announces ministry name, then news headings (e.g., "Università, Bernini..."), "In evidenza", "IL MINISTRO".
- Insert+F7 (links list): Select from "Vai alla sezione" for biography/statements/multimedia, or news links like funding details.
- R for regions/landmarks jumps to "main" content (news/highlights), "navigation" (menu), "contentinfo" (footer with site map/accessibility).
- Up/down arrows or page down reads sequentially: Header → news → Minister profile → footer links.[2][11]
