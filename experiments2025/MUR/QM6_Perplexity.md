## Availability of Competitions Information

Yes, the homepage provides indirect access to competitions ("concorsi") via the main navigation menu and related sections like "Ministero" or "Concorsi e Avvisi," linking to detailed listings of public competitions, mobility notices, and expert selections for ministry roles.[2][11][12]

## Visual Navigation Steps

- Locate the top navigation bar below the header; select "Ministero" (Ministry) menu item.
- Within submenus or linked pages, find "Concorsi e Avvisi" (Competitions and Notices) for listings like Avviso n. 1141 (6 Nov 2025, 10 positions), Avviso n. 8875 (Jun 2025), and older concours for 125 non-managerial staff.
- Click highlighted news or "In Evidenza" links mentioning decrees (e.g., Decreto Ministeriale) that authorize 2025 competitions for ~80 functionaries.[1][11][2]

## Screen Reader Navigation Steps

- Press H to jump to "Ministero" heading, then Tab or Enter to activate the menu link.
- Insert+F7 for links list; search/type "concorsi" or "avvisi" to jump to "Concorsi e Avvisi" or notices like "Avviso n. 1141 del 6-11-2025."
- In main content, H keys reach news on funding/decrees tying to competitions; R for regions to "navigation" then arrow through subitems.[11][12][2]
