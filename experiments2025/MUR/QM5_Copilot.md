Yes — the homepage of the Ministry of University and Research (https://www.mur.gov.it/it) includes a section specifically for **recently published laws and decrees**.  

---

## 🖥️ Visual description

- Scroll down the page until you reach the section titled **“Atti e normativa”**.  
- In this section, you’ll see a list of the most recent decrees. Each item shows:  
  - The decree number.  
  - The date of publication.  
  - The subject area.  
- Examples currently visible on the page:  
  - **Decreto Dirigenziale n. 18169 del 17-11-2025** – Social Sciences and Humanities.  
  - **Decreto Dirigenziale n. 18010 del 12-11-2025** – Physical Sciences and Engineering.  
- Each decree title is a clickable link that opens the full text.

---

## 🎧 Screen reader workflow

1. Use the **`H` key** to move through headings until you reach **“Atti e normativa”**.  
2. Below this heading, your screen reader will announce each decree as a **link**. For example:  
   - “Decreto Dirigenziale n. 18169 del 17-11-2025 – Scienze Sociali e Umanistiche, link”.  
   - “Decreto Dirigenziale n. 18010 del 12-11-2025 – Scienze Fisiche e Ingegneria, link”.  
3. Press **Enter** on any decree link to open the full law text.  
4. Continue with `Tab` or `Down Arrow` to move through the list if more decrees are available.
