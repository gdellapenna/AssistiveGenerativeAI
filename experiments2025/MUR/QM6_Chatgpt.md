Yes — the **MUR website** provides access to information on competitions, such as calls for research positions, academic competitions, or selection procedures for universities and research institutions. Here’s how you can access it from the **[https://www.mur.gov.it/it](https://www.mur.gov.it/it)** page.

---

## **1. Visual / Sighted Navigation**

1. Go to the **top menu** or scroll down the home page.
2. Look for the section **“Atti e normativa”** (Acts and regulations).

   * This section often lists decrees, official announcements, and **competition calls**.
3. Click on **“Atti e normativa”**.
4. On the resulting page, scan for items labeled as:

   * “Bandi di concorso” (competition notices)
   * “Avvisi pubblici” (public notices)
   * “Selezioni” (selections)
5. Click on any title to open the full document or PDF containing the details of the competition, deadlines, and requirements.

Some competitions may also be highlighted in **news / “In evidenza”** if they are recent or urgent.

---

## **2. Screen Reader Navigation**

1. After opening **[https://www.mur.gov.it/it](https://www.mur.gov.it/it)**, jump to the **main navigation landmark** or use heading navigation (`H`) until you reach **“Atti e normativa”**.
2. Press Enter to open the page.
3. Once on the “Atti e normativa” page, use link or heading navigation:

   * Look for links labeled **“Bandi di concorso”**, **“Avvisi pubblici”**, or **“Selezioni”**.
   * Each link represents a competition notice.
4. Activate a link (Enter) to access the full document.

   * Usually, this is a PDF or an HTML page with:

     * Title of the competition
     * Deadline
     * Eligibility requirements
     * How to apply
5. If needed, use the “Back” function or link navigation to return to the full list of competitions.

---

✅ **Summary:**

* Competitions are **listed under “Atti e normativa”**, sometimes highlighted in news.
* Items to look for: **Bandi di concorso, Avvisi pubblici, Selezioni**.
* Every item is a **clickable link** leading to the full details of the competition.
