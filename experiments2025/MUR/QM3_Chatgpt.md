## 📋 Principali menu visibili sulla home page

When you open the home page, you see (near the top) a main navigation menu. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

These menu‑options include:

* **Governo Italiano** — a link to the overall Government site. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Ministero dell’Università e della Ricerca** (i.e. MUR home) ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Search** (an icon / field to search the site) ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Università** — one of the top‑level thematic areas. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Ricerca** — another top‑level thematic area (for research). ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Afam** — the area for “AFAM” (Alta Formazione Artistica, Musicale e Coreutica). ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

Also there is a “Menu” button (or link) at top (presumably to open a full site‑menu / navigation panel). ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

## 🧮 What’s inside / sub‑menus — big menu hierarchy

If you access the full navigation (e.g. via the “Menu”), the site provides a broad hierarchical menu structure. Some of the principal branches and their sub‑items are: ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][2])

### **Ministero** (the institutional part)

Under this you find:

* **Il Ministro** — with sub‑pages: “Attività”, “Dichiarazioni”, “Multimedia”. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Missione e funzione** — the mission and functions of the Ministry. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Concorsi e Avvisi** — competitions / public calls, notices. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Consultazioni pubbliche** — public consultations. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Sedi e strutture** — addresses and organizational structures. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* **Organizzazione** — internal organization, with many sub‑divisions: e.g. “Uffici di diretta collaborazione MUR”, “Articolazione degli uffici MUR” etc. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

### **Aree tematiche** (Thematic areas / policy areas)

This is perhaps the most extensive menu. Inside “Aree tematiche” there are at least three big branches:

* **Università** — with many sub‑items:

  * Types of universities (statali / non statali / telematiche / special‑status, etc.) ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * Offerings: degrees (Lauree, Lauree Magistrali), Master, Dottorati, Scuole di specializzazione, etc. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * Student‑related pages: diritto allo studio, residenze, mobilità internazionale, accesso alle professioni, riconoscimenti di titoli, etc. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * Docenti universitari, carriera accademica, reclutamento, abilitazioni, concorsi, organi ausiliari (es. organi di rappresentanza) ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * Programmazione e finanziamenti, bilanci, dipartimenti di eccellenza, statistiche, sportello digitale, riconoscimento titoli esteri, etc. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

* **Ricerca** — under this branch you find items about the national research system, public research institutions, normative for research, funding, projects, evaluation, international research, etc. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

* **Afam** — inside AFAM you have sub‑menus like:

  * “Gli Istituti” (the institutions) — list of all AFAM‑institutions. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Offerta formativa (Titoli rilasciati)” — degrees and titles issued by AFAM institutions. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Spazio europeo della formazione superiore” — info about European Higher Education Area insofar relevant to AFAM. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Spettacoli e mostre AFAM” — information about performances and exhibitions. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Premi per giovani artisti” — awards for young artists. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Docenti e altro personale AFAM” — staffing / staff‑related for AFAM institutions. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Organi ausiliari del sistema AFAM” — auxiliary bodies of the AFAM system. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Conferenze Istituzioni AFAM” — conferences / institutional events of AFAM. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Rilevazioni statistiche” — statistical data concerning AFAM. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
  * “Bandi Dottorati AFAM” — calls / announcements for AFAM doctoral funding or access. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])

At the bottom (footer) there is also a menu with links like:

## 🎯 What this means / how menus are organized

* The site’s navigation is hierarchical: top‑level menu for the main “domains” (Ministero, Università, Ricerca, Afam), then each domain expands into many sub‑pages.
* The “Aree tematiche” section organizes all the policy / academic / research‑system areas — especially useful if you want to find information about universities, degree programmes, research institutions, AFAM, etc.
* If you scroll down to the footer you get a full site map / expanded menu — useful to jump to sub‑sections deep in the hierarchy.

## 🔉 What a screen‑reader user would see as “menus”

If you use a screen reader:

* At the top you’ll encounter a “Menu” link/button. Activating it (e.g. pressing Enter) should open the site’s navigation menu. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* Then you’ll find top‑level links (like “Università”, “Ricerca”, “Afam”, “Ministero”, “Governo Italiano”) — you can navigate among them using link navigation (e.g. next‑link). ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
* When you follow one of those top‑level links (e.g. “Università”), the page will contain sub‑links to sub‑sections — you can navigate them likewise. ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][3])
* The footer also contains many links (site‑map, privacy, etc.), accessible via link navigation once you scroll to bottom (or use landmark navigation, if supported) ([Ministero Infrastrutture e Trasporti][1])
