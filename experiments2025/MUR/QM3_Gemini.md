### Menu Options Description

The main menu is accessible via the word **"Menu"** near the top of the page. Once opened, it shows a list of main categories. Most categories have a sub-menu indicated by the words **"Open submenu"** next to them.

The main menu options are:

#### **Screen Reader Directions (How your reader finds the elements):**

1.  **Locate the Menu Button:** Use the $\text{TAB}$ key or your screen reader's button shortcut ($\text{B}$) to find the button labeled **"Menu"**.
2.  **Open the Menu:** Press $\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$ on the "Menu" button to expand the navigation structure.
3.  **Navigate Main Links:** Use the link shortcut ($\text{L}$) or $\text{TAB}$ key to navigate through the main link items. Note that almost every link is followed by the text **"Open submenu"** (e.g., "Ministero, Open submenu"). This indicates that pressing $\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$ on that link will expand a second level of navigation.
4.  **The main links you will hear are:**
    * `Home` (Link)
    * `Ministero` (Link, Open submenu)
    * `Aree tematiche` (Link, Open submenu)
    * `Atti e normativa` (Link, Open submenu)
    * `Portale dei dati dell'istruzione superiore` (Link)
    * ...and the remaining links listed in the table above.

To access details within a section, such as what's under "Ministero" (Ministry), you would first interact with the "Ministero" link (using $\text{ENTER}$ or $\text{SPACE}$) and then navigate the newly revealed sub-links.
