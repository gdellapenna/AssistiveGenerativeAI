Here’s how to search for a specific book on the Amazon.com homepage:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users (Visual Instructions)

1. **Locate the search bar** at the top center of the page.
2. To the left of the search bar, click the **dropdown menu** labeled “All” to open a list of departments.
3. Scroll through the list and select **“Books”** or **“Kindle Books”** depending on your preference.
4. In the search bar, **type the title or author** of the book you’re looking for.
5. Click the **magnifying glass icon** or press **Enter** to begin the search.
6. You’ll be taken to a results page showing matching books.

---

### 🧭 For Screen Reader Users (Accessible Instructions)

1. Use **Tab navigation** to reach the **search form** near the top of the page.
2. First, locate the **combo box** labeled “Search in” or “Search category.” It may say “All Departments.”
3. Activate the combo box and use **arrow keys** to select **“Books”** or **“Kindle Store.”**
4. Tab to the **search input field** and type the **book title or author**.
5. Tab to the **Search button** (labeled “Go” or “Submit”) and activate it with **Enter**.
6. You’ll be redirected to a results page with book listings.
