Yes — there are multiple menus on the Amazon homepage. Here’s a breakdown of the main ones I can identify from the page, with how to access them visually and via screen-reader.

---

### 1. Top navigation / header menu

**Visible items:**

* “Deliver to [location]” or “Select your address”.
* “Hello, Sign in / Account & Lists”.
* “Returns & Orders”.
* “Cart” icon.
* A large search bar (input field) with category dropdown (often labelled “All”).
* To the left of the search bar a category selector (for example “All”).
* On many screens there may also be a “Hamburger” menu icon (three horizontal lines) or “All” that expands a list of departments.

**Screen-reader instructions:**

* Press `Ctrl + Home` to go to the top of the page.
* Tab through until you hear “Deliver to … link”, “Hello, Sign in link”, “Accounts & Lists menu”, “Returns & Orders link”, then “Cart link”.
* Continue until you locate “All departments drop-down” (just before the search field) and then “Search Amazon edit” (the input field) and “Search button”.
* To use the “All departments” drop-down: when you hear “All departments drop-down”, press `Enter` or `Space` to expand it, then Tab through the list of department names.

---

### 2. Departments / “All” menu (often hidden behind the “hamburger” icon)

**Visible items:**
If you click (or hover) the “All” or “☰” (hamburger) icon / text near the top-left, a panel opens listing major product categories, for example:

* “Amazon Devices”
* “TV & Video”
* “Computers”
* “Smart Home”
* “Audio & Headphones”
* “Wearable Technology”
* “Video Games”
* “Movies & TV”
* “Books”
* “Gift Cards & Amazon Wallet”
* etc.
  (Exact list may vary by locale and user status.)

**Screen-reader instructions:**

* At the top of the page, tab until you hear “All departments link” or “Menu icon button”. Press `Enter` or `Space` to open the menu.
* Then use `Tab` (or arrow keys if your reader supports) to navigate through each category item. You’ll hear each category as: “[Category name] link”.
* To select a category, tab to it and press `Enter`.

---

### 3. Footer menu(s)

**Visible items:**
At the bottom of the page there is a section of links organised into groups. Typical group headings include:

* “Make Money with Us”
* “Let Us Help You”
* “Amazon Payment Products”
* “Get to Know Us”
  Under each heading are links such as:

  * “Careers”
  * “About Amazon”
  * “Press Releases”
  * “Help” / “Customer Service”
  * “Gift Cards”
  * “Sell on Amazon”
  * “Amazon Business”
  * etc.

**Screen-reader instructions:**

* Press `End` to jump to the bottom of the page (or repeatedly press `Page Down`).
* Use heading navigation (`H`) until you hear a heading like “Let Us Help You” or “Get to Know Us”.
* Under that heading, use arrow or Tab to hear the list items (e.g., “Help link”, “Careers link”, etc.).
* To follow one, tab to the link and press `Enter`.
