Here’s how to contact Amazon Customer Service directly from the homepage:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users (Visual Instructions)

1. **Scroll to the very bottom of the page** until you reach the **footer section**.
2. Look for a column titled **“Let Us Help You”**.
3. In that column, click the link labeled **“Help”**. It’s usually the first or second link in that section.
4. This will take you to the Amazon Help & Customer Service page, where you can:
   - Browse help topics
   - Contact support via chat or phone
   - Track orders or returns

---

### 🧭 For Screen Reader Users (Accessible Instructions)

1. Press the **End** key or use your screen reader’s shortcut to jump to the **footer region**.
2. Navigate by **heading** or **link navigation** until you find the section labeled **“Let Us Help You”**.
3. Use the arrow keys or Tab to locate the link named **“Help”**.
   - It is a standard HTML link with the accessible name “Help”.
4. Activate the link (usually **Enter** or **Spacebar**) to go to the Help & Customer Service page.
5. Once there, use heading navigation to explore topics or locate the **“Contact Us”** option.
