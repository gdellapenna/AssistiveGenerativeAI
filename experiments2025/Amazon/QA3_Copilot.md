Yes, the Amazon.com homepage contains several menus. Here's a detailed breakdown of all the menu options currently available:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users (Visual Layout)

#### 1. **Top Navigation Bar**
Located at the very top of the page:
- **Deliver to [Location]**: Sets your delivery address.
- **Language Selector**: Changes site language.
- **Account & Lists**: Opens a dropdown with options like Sign In, Your Account, Orders, Recommendations.
- **Returns & Orders**: Takes you to your order history.
- **Cart**: Shows items in your shopping cart.
- **Search Bar**: Lets you search for products.

#### 2. **Main Horizontal Menu**
Just below the search bar, labeled “All”:
- Clicking **“All”** opens a **side menu** with categories such as:
  - Amazon Home
  - Prime Video
  - Amazon Music
  - Echo & Alexa
  - Fire Tablets
  - Kindle E-readers
  - Books
  - Toys & Games
  - Fashion
  - Beauty & Personal Care
  - Sports & Outdoors
  - Automotive
  - Grocery & Gourmet Food
  - Pet Supplies
  - Gift Cards
  - Pharmacy
  - Subscribe & Save
  - Customer Service

#### 3. **Promotional Menus**
Mid-page banners and sections include:
- “Holiday Gift Guides”
- “Shop by Category”
- “Deals on Tech”
- “Shop by Interest”

These are not dropdowns but clickable sections that lead to curated product lists.

---

### 🧭 For Screen Reader Users (Accessible Structure)

#### 1. **Top Navigation Region**
Use Tab or landmark navigation to reach:
- **Link: Deliver to [Location]**
- **Button: Language Selector**
- **Link: Account & Lists** (Expands to submenu with links like Sign In, Your Account, etc.)
- **Link: Returns & Orders**
- **Link: Cart**
- **Search Form**: Includes a combo box for category selection and a text input for search.

#### 2. **Main Menu (Hamburger Menu labeled “All”)**
- Navigate to the **button labeled “All”**.
- Activate it to open a **side navigation menu**.
- Use arrow keys or Tab to explore categories like:
  - Prime Video
  - Amazon Music
  - Echo & Alexa
  - Books
  - Fashion
  - Beauty
  - Grocery
  - Pharmacy
  - Customer Service

Each item is a link with a clear accessible name.

#### 3. **Main Content Sections**
Use heading navigation (`H` key) to jump between:
- “Holiday Gift Guides”
- “Deals on Tech”
- “Shop by Interest”
- “Best Sellers”
- “Top Picks for You”

These are structured as headings followed by product carousels or links.
