To restrict your search to books only on the Amazon homepage, follow these steps:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users (Visual Instructions)

1. **Go to the top center of the page** where the **search bar** is located.
2. To the **left of the search box**, click the **dropdown menu** labeled **“All”**.
3. A list of departments will appear. **Scroll down** and click on **“Books”** or **“Kindle Store”** depending on your preference.
4. Now type your search term (e.g., book title or author) into the search box.
5. Click the **magnifying glass icon** or press **Enter** to search.
6. The results will now be limited to the selected book category.

---

### 🧭 For Screen Reader Users (Accessible Instructions)

1. Use **Tab navigation** to reach the **search form** near the top of the page.
2. The first element is a **combo box** labeled something like **“Search in”** or **“All Departments”**.
3. Activate the combo box with **Enter** or **Spacebar**.
4. Use the **arrow keys** to navigate through the list and select **“Books”** or **“Kindle Store”**.
5. Press **Enter** to confirm your selection.
6. Tab to the **search input field**, type your search query, then Tab to the **Search button** and press **Enter**.

This ensures your search results will only include books. Let me know if you’d like help searching for a specific title or navigating the results.
