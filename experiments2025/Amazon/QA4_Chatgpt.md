Yes — there is a way to log in on the Amazon homepage. Here’s **how** to do it, with both visual instructions and screen-reader instructions.

---

### Visual Instructions

1. At the top of the page (in the header area) look for a link labelled **“Hello, Sign in”** (or similar wording) near where it shows your account/address.
2. Click that “Sign in” link.
3. You will be taken to the login page where you can enter your email address or mobile phone number, then your password. ([Amazon][1])
4. After entering your credentials, click the “Continue” or “Sign-In” button to access your account.

---

### Screen-Reader Instructions

1. From anywhere on the page press `Ctrl + Home` (Windows) or use the screen-reader’s “go to top” command to reach the top of the page.
2. Use `Tab` repeatedly (or your screen-reader’s “next link” command) until you hear something like “Hello, Sign in link”.
3. When you hear that, activate it by pressing `Enter`.
4. On the login page you’ll hear “Email or mobile phone number edit” (or similar)—enter your email or phone number.
5. Press `Tab` to reach the “Continue” button (or equivalent) and press `Enter`.
6. Then you’ll be prompted for “Password edit” — type your password.
7. Press `Tab` to get to “Sign-In button” (or similar) and press `Enter`.
8. After that you should be signed into your Amazon account.
