**Visual description:**
It’s located near the **top centre** of the page, just below the main header. You’ll see:

* a drop-down (often labelled “All” or “All Departments”) to the left of the search input field.
* the search input box (placeholder text like “Search Amazon” or similar) immediately to the right of that drop-down.
* then to the right of the input field a **Search** button (magnifying glass icon).

**Screen‐reader instructions:**

1. From anywhere on the page press `Ctrl + Home` (Windows) or use your screen-reader’s “go to top” command to reach the top of the page.
2. Use `Tab` until you hear something like: “All departments drop-down button”.
3. Continue pressing `Tab` once more until you hear: “Search Amazon edit”. That is the search input field.
4. Press `Tab` again until you hear: “Search button” (or “Go” button).
