Here’s how to locate and use the search bar on the Amazon.com homepage:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users (Visual Instructions)

- The **search bar is located at the top center** of the page, just below the Amazon logo.
- It’s a long horizontal box with a dropdown menu on the left labeled **“All”**, a text input field in the middle, and a **magnifying glass icon** on the right.
- You can:
  1. Click the dropdown to choose a department (e.g., Books, Electronics).
  2. Click inside the text box to type your search.
  3. Click the magnifying glass or press Enter to search.

---

### 🧭 For Screen Reader Users (Accessible Instructions)

- Use **Tab navigation** or **landmark navigation** to reach the **search form**, which is in the **navigation region** near the top of the page.
- The search form includes:
  1. A **combo box** labeled “Search in” or “Search category” (default is “All Departments”). Use arrow keys to select a category like “Books.”
  2. A **text input field** labeled “Search.” Type your query here.
  3. A **Search button** labeled “Go” or “Submit.” Activate it with Enter to perform the search.
