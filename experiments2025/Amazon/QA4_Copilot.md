Yes, you can log in to your Amazon account directly from the homepage. Here's how to do it:

---

### 🖥️ For Sighted Users (Visual Instructions)

1. **Look at the top-right corner** of the page.
2. Find the section labeled **“Account & Lists”**. It may say “Hello, sign in” above it.
3. **Hover your mouse over** this section. A dropdown menu will appear.
4. Click the **“Sign in”** button at the top of the dropdown.
5. You’ll be taken to the login page where you can enter your email and password.

---

### 🧭 For Screen Reader Users (Accessible Instructions)

1. Use **Tab navigation** or **landmark navigation** to reach the **navigation region** at the top of the page.
2. Tab through until you hear **“Account & Lists”** or **“Hello, sign in”**.
3. This is a **button with a dropdown menu**. Activate it with **Enter** or **Spacebar**.
4. The first item in the dropdown is **“Sign in”**. Use arrow keys or Tab to reach it.
5. Activate the **“Sign in”** link to go to the login page.
6. On the login page, use form navigation to enter your email and password.
