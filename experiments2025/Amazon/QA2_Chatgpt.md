Here’s how you can contact Amazon customer service from the homepage ([http://www.amazon.com](http://www.amazon.com)) — first as a visual-guide, then how to do it with a screen reader.

---

### Visual instructions

1. On the homepage, scroll down to the very bottom of the page (until you reach the footer area).
2. In the footer, look for a link labelled **“Customer Service”** or **“Help”**. Click it.
3. On the “Help & Contact Us” page, you’ll see options for topics like “Returns & refunds”, “Shipping & Delivery”, etc. Also look for a button or link labelled **“Contact Us”**.
4. Clicking “Contact Us” will present you with contact methods such as live chat, email form, or a phone number (in the U.S. it is 1-888-280-4331). ([Amazon][1])
5. Choose your preferred method (e.g., phone call or chat) and follow prompts to proceed.

---

### Screen‐reader instructions

1. Press `Ctrl + Home` to move to the top of the page. Then use the shortcut for “go to end of page” — e.g., `End` on Windows — to jump to the footer.
2. Once you reach the footer area, use the Tab key or your screen reader’s “next link” command (for example NVDA: `Insert + F7` to open links list) until you hear “Customer Service link” or “Help link”. Activate it by pressing `Enter`.
3. On the Help page, use the heading list command (`H` in many screen readers) to find the heading “Help & Contact Us.”
4. Under that heading, tab through or use the link list to locate “Contact Us” and activate it.
5. You will then hear something like “Contact us. How would you like us to contact you? Chat / Phone / Email”. Use Tab to navigate to the phone number (you should hear “Phone number 1-888-280-4331”) or “Chat” link. Press `Enter` on your chosen method.
6. If you chose phone, dial the number. If chat, your screen reader will announce the chat interface or pop-up; follow the chat prompts.
7. Have ready any information you might need (order number, account email) because you may be asked for it.
