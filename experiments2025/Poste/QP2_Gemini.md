I can certainly describe the structure of the Poste Italiane homepage for you, assuming you have dismissed the initial cookie banner. The page is organized into a few major areas: a persistent Header for navigation and utility links, a very large main content area dominated by product information, carousels, and quick tools, and a comprehensive Footer.

### **1. Standard Visual Directions (Page Structure)**

The page is structured as follows, from top to bottom:

1.  **Header (Top of the Page):** This area contains the main brand logo and links for general utility and main navigation.
    * **Contextual Links:** Links like "Privati," "Business," "Premium," "Chi siamo," and "TG Poste" (Private, Business, Premium, Who we are, TG Poste).
    * **Utility Links:** Links for "Assistenza," "Bacheca," and "Area personale" (Assistance, Mailbox, Personal Area).
    * **Main Menu (Mega Menu):** A large "Menu" button opens a wide-ranging list of products and services, categorized under titles like "Posta e pacchi," "Conti e invio denaro," "Carte," "Finanziamenti," etc.

2.  **Main Content Area (The Body of the Page):** This section is highly dynamic and scrollable, segmented by clear headings (H2 elements).
    * **MyPoste (Personal Area):** A box labeled "Area Personale MyPoste" with an "Accedi" (Access/Login) button.
    * **Product/Service Sections (The Core Navigation):** The rest of the page is a long list of major product categories, each presented with a prominent heading, such as:
        * "Posta e pacchi" (Mail and packages)
        * "Conti e invio denaro" (Accounts and money transfer)
        * "Carte" (Cards)
        * "Finanziamenti" (Financing)
        * ...and so on, covering **Risparmio Postale**, **Investimenti**, **Assicurazioni e previdenza**, **Energia**, **Telefonia**, **Filatelia**, and **Servizi al cittadino e SPID**.
    * **Tool Sections:** Areas for quick actions, including:
        * A carousel of promotional offers (e.g., "Buono Premium 4 anni").
        * A section for searching: **"Cerca spedizioni"** (Track shipments) and **"Cerca un ufficio e prenota"** (Find an office and book an appointment).
        * A carousel of **"Servizi in evidenza"** (Featured Services).
        * A section for **"Offerte e promozioni"** (Offers and promotions).
    * **News:** A "News" section with recent articles.

3.  **Footer (Bottom of the Page):** Contains an extensive list of non-product links, categorized for easy reference. The main categories here are repeated:
    * **Area personale** (Personal Area)
    * **Assistenza** (Assistance)
    * **Canali** (Channels)
    * **Accessibilità** (Accessibility)
    * **Normative** (Regulations)
    * **L'azienda** (The Company)
    * **Altri siti** (Other Sites)

### **2. Screen Reader Directions (Page Structure Navigation)**

You can navigate this page efficiently using standard screen reader shortcuts:

1.  **Skip Navigation:** When the page loads, you should hear a link or button that says **"Passa al contenuto principale"** (Skip to main content). Press **Enter** on this to bypass the header and immediately reach the important content.

2.  **Navigate by Headings (H2):** The most effective way to understand the structure is to use the heading navigation shortcut (usually the **H** key). This allows you to jump between the major sections of the page:
    * **Area Personale MyPoste**
    * **Posta e pacchi**
    * **Conti e invio denaro**
    * **Carte**
    * **Finanziamenti**
    * **Risparmio Postale**
    * **Investimenti**
    * **Assicurazioni e previdenza**
    * **Energia**
    * **Telefonia**
    * **Filatelia**
    * **Servizi al cittadino e SPID**
    * **TG Poste** (Near the beginning of the body content)
    * **News** (Near the bottom)

3.  **Find Navigation Links:**
    * The main site navigation is accessible using the **"Menu"** button, located in the header. To find it quickly, you can typically use the **Tab** key from the top of the page.
    * The secondary utility links ("Assistenza," "Area personale," etc.) are near the top, before the "Menu" button.

4.  **Find Quick Tools (Forms):** To find the search or tracking tools, you can use the **F** key to jump to form fields or input boxes, looking for things like "Inserisci codice spedizione" (Enter shipment code) or "Cerca un Ufficio Postale o un Punto Poste" (Find a Post Office or a Punto Poste).

5.  **Access Footer:** You can jump to the bottom of the page or use the link **"Passa al footer di pagina"** (Skip to page footer) near the top to find the extensive assistance and regulatory links.
