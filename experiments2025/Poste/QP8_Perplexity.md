## Main App: Poste Italiane
Download the "Poste Italiane" app from Google Play or Apple App Store. It consolidates services including BancoPosta accounts, Postepay management, parcel tracking and rescheduling, mail sending, post office bookings, bill payments, and telephony for PosteMobile. Log in with PosteID credentials for personalized access mirroring website features.[1][2][8]

## Additional App: PosteID
The "PosteID" app handles digital identity (SPID-enabled) for secure logins across services, with electronic document registration for online verification. It supports quick access to public and private services linked to Poste Italiane offerings.[5]

## Screen Reader Compatibility
Both apps feature voiceover support; search app stores using screen reader navigation for "Poste Italiane" or "PosteID," review accessibility ratings (4.4+ stars), and enable push notifications for alerts on shipments or appointments. Customize the home screen for frequent tasks like tracking.[7][11][1]
