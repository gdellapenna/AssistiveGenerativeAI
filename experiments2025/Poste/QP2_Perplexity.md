The structure of the Poste.it website as of late 2025 is designed to optimize user experience through a modern, modular layout that reflects Poste Italiane's broader strategic transformation toward a digital and omnichannel platform.

The page consists of clearly defined sections: a prominent main banner area highlighting current financial products and loyalty bonuses; a section focused on "Offerte e promozioni" (Offers and Promotions) with particular emphasis on energy services from Poste Energia; and a "News" section presenting up-to-date corporate communications like financial results and social initiatives, each with clear date stamps to organize the information chronologically.

Navigation is streamlined into a slim top menu bar presenting the main service categories including postal, financial, insurance, and digital services. This menu grants easy tab and focus access for keyboard and screen reader users. Additional key information areas such as "My Poste" for personalized account management and the integration of recognizable icons contribute to usability and quick service access.

The website’s structure supports accessibility with semantic HTML, landmarks, ARIA labels on icons and buttons, and text-based headings to guide screen reader navigation efficiently through major content areas and interactive elements. This aligns with Poste Italiane’s strategic goal of transforming physical post offices and the website into relational, customer-centered digital service hubs with intelligent and customized user experiences.

In summary, Poste.it combines clear visual hierarchy and effective semantic markup to present offers, news, and services with a user-friendly, accessible interface that supports both sighted navigation and screen reader interaction seamlessly.[1][11][12]
