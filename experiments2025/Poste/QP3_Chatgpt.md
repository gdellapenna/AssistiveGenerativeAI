### 🎯 What this means for navigation / accessibility

* Since there is no slider, you can navigate page content in a linear, predictable way: heading by heading, block by block.

* Each image or section is a separate item: you can move through them using headings, links, or standard navigation commands, without needing to detect a changing carousel.
