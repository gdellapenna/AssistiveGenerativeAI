Yes, the Poste.it homepage contains a search bar. It allows you to search for various items including:

- Your shipments or packages by entering tracking numbers.
- Postal codes (CAP) or nearby post boxes.
- Products and services offered by Poste Italiane for private individuals and families.

The search function is designed to help quickly locate specific postal or package information, as well as navigate the range of services and products available on the site.

For a screen reader user, the search bar is typically found near the top of the page, announced as a search form with an input field where you can type keywords related to shipments, products, or postal services. After entering your query, you can submit the search by pressing the Enter key or activating the associated search button.

This search tool is an integral part of the homepage, supporting access to tracking, locators, and service/product details.[5][6][8][11]
