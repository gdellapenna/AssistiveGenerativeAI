Yes — you can **track the status of a shipment directly from the Poste Italiane home page**. ([poste.it](https://www.poste.it/))

Here’s how it works:

---

## 🔹 How to track a shipment visually

1. Look for the section labelled **“Cerca spedizioni”** (“Track shipments”). This is usually a **text input field** prominently displayed on the home page, sometimes near the top or in a sidebar.
2. Enter your **tracking code** (alphanumeric code from your shipping receipt).
3. Click or tap the **“Avvia la ricerca”** button (“Start search”).
4. The site will load a page showing the current status of your shipment, including where it is, expected delivery, and any updates.

---

## 🧑‍🦯 Screen-Reader-Friendly Steps

1. Use your screen reader to navigate to a **text field** labelled:

   > “Inserisci codice spedizione” (Enter shipment code)
2. Type in the tracking code exactly as it appears on your receipt.
3. Navigate to the **button** labelled:

   > “Avvia la ricerca” (Start search)
4. Press **Enter**.
5. The resulting page will announce information about your shipment, including:

   * Current location/status
   * Expected delivery date
   * History of shipment events

---

### ✅ Notes

* You do **not need to log in** to track most shipments; the tracking code alone is sufficient.
* If you want updates for multiple shipments, you can repeat the process with different codes.
* Some shipments (like registered mail or financial products) might require login for full details, but standard parcels usually show status with just the tracking code.
