## ✅ Which apps exist / are available

* The main app for consumers is App Poste Italiane. ([Poste.it][1])
* There is also a business‑oriented app: App PosteBusiness — for users with business/enterprise services. ([Poste Italiane][2])

Notably, from **9 October 2025** the main “App Poste Italiane” replaced older separate apps (such as the former banking app App BancoPosta and the former payment/card‑focused app App Postepay). ([Corriere Nazionale][3])

---

## 📲 What you can do with the app

Using “App Poste Italiane,” you can:

* Manage bank accounts and cards, e.g. check balances, transfer money, pay bills, top up SIM or prepaid cards. ([Poste.it][1])
* Send and track parcels or mail, manage shipments, schedule deliveries or pickup, etc. ([Poste.it][1])
* Pay services like utilities, handle energy and telephone services if you are a customer. ([Poste.it][1])
* Manage savings, investments, insurance, and other financial or postal‑related services offered by Poste. ([Poste.it][1])
* Book appointments at post offices, find nearest offices/ATMs, handle postal services more conveniently from smartphone. ([App Store][4])
