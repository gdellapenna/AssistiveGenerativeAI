Yes — there *are* apps you can download to access many of the services described on the Poste Italiane website. ([Poste.it][1])

### 📱 Which app and what it does

* The main app is App Poste Italiane. Since 2025 it replaces previous apps such as PostePay and BancoPosta, unifying services into a single application. ([Poste.it][2])
* With App Poste Italiane you can access many services: manage bank/ prepaid accounts, handle payments and transfers, track shipments and parcels, pay bills, recharge mobile SIM (if you have a PosteMobile line), manage energy/utility services, request financial or insurance products, locate offices/ATMs, and more. ([Poste.it][1])

### ✅ Key points about the app

* App Poste Italiane is free and available for both iOS and Android. ([Poste.it][2])
* You don’t need a new account if you already have credentials on the website — you can log in with the same credentials (or use your existing Postepay/BancoPosta credentials). ([Poste.it][2])
* The app consolidates many services, making it likely the easiest way to access your account, track parcels, manage payments or cards, and so forth. ([Poste.it][1])
