Yes, the Poste.it homepage features a slim, modular main navigation menu at the top, designed for simple and accessible browsing across services.

## Menu Structure
The menu organizes options into key categories for postal, financial, and digital services:
- **Pagamenti online** (Online Payments): Access bill payments and transfers.
- **Spedisci pacco online** (Send Package Online): Initiate parcel shipping.
- **Spedisci posta online** (Send Mail Online): Handle letters and registered mail.
- **Offerta luce e gas** (Energy Offers): Explore Poste Energia plans.
- **Check-up assicurativo** (Insurance Check-up): Review insurance products.
- **Conto BancoPosta** (BancoPosta Account): Banking services.
- **Libretti di Risparmio Postale** (Postal Savings Books): Savings options.

A common transversal navigation bar supports quick shifts between consumer and business sites, with icons and text labels for familiarity.[1][7]

## Screen Reader Navigation
Use heading (H1-H2) or landmark navigation to reach the "navigation" region or main menu landmark at the page top. Tab through links announced by category names like "Pagamenti online" or icons with ARIA labels (e.g., "Spedisci pacco"). Submenus expand on focus for detailed options; keyboard arrows or Enter activate selections.[4][11][1]
