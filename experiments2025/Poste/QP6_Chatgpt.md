Yes — the Poste Italiane home page **does provide a way to log in**. ([poste.it](https://www.poste.it/))

Here’s the breakdown:

---

## 🔑 Login Options on the Home Page

1. **Area Personale MyPoste / Accedi**

   * There is a button or link labelled **“Accedi”** or **“Area Personale MyPoste”** prominently near the top of the page.
   * This is the main login gateway for private users to access their accounts, manage payments, track parcels, check banking services, and more.

2. **Services requiring login**

   * Certain actions, like checking your shipments in detail, accessing financial accounts, or managing SPID, will prompt you to log in through this same “Accedi” button.

3. **Login methods**

   * You can log in using:

     * **PosteID / SPID** (digital identity)
     * Username and password for your MyPoste account
     * Other authentication methods offered in the login interface

---

## 🧑‍🦯 Screen-Reader-Friendly Steps to Log In

1. Navigate the page by landmarks or links to locate **“Accedi”** or **“Area Personale MyPoste”**. Your screen reader should announce something like:

   > “Button: Accedi” or “Link: Area Personale MyPoste”

2. Press **Enter** on the link/button. This will open the login page or popup.

3. On the login interface:

   * Move to the text field labelled **Username / Email / PosteID** and type your username.
   * Move to the field labelled **Password** and type your password.
   * Navigate to the **Login / Accedi** button and press Enter.

4. If you have SPID, there will be a separate option to select SPID authentication and follow its prompts.
