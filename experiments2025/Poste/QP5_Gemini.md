Yes, the page provides multiple ways to contact Poste Italiane, primarily located in two key areas: within a hidden assistance bar near the top of the page, and within the main footer at the bottom.

### **1. Assistance Bar (Near the top of the page)**

There is a section for quick help that offers contact methods.

#### **Standard Visual Directions**

Near the very top of the page, there is a small label or button that says "**Apri la barra di assistenza**" (Open the assistance bar). When this is opened, a panel appears titled "**Ciao, Come possiamo aiutarti?**" (Hello, How can we help you?).

Near the bottom of this panel, under the heading "**Contatta il nostro servizio clienti**" (Contact our customer service), there is a button labeled:

* **Chiamaci** (Call us) - *This leads to phone contact.*

#### **Screen Reader Directions**

1.  From the top of the page, use the **Tab** key until you hear the element labeled **"Apri la barra di assistenza"** (Open the assistance bar). Press **Enter** or **Space** to activate it.
2.  Navigate through the content of the opened bar. You can use the **H** key to find the section for assistance.
3.  Listen for the link or button labeled **"Chiamaci"** (Call us). Press **Enter** to initiate the contact process.

### **2. Footer (Bottom of the page)**

The footer contains a comprehensive section dedicated to assistance.

#### **Standard Visual Directions**

Scroll all the way to the bottom of the page (the Footer). Look for the heading "**Assistenza**" (Assistance). Under this heading, you will find several links detailing contact methods:

* **Domande frequenti** (Frequently asked questions)
* **Chiamaci** (Call us)
* **Entra in chat** (Enter chat)
* **Scrivici** (Write to us)
* **Reclami e Conciliazione** (Complaints and Conciliation)
* **Postemyvoice riconoscimento vocale** (Postemyvoice voice recognition)

#### **Screen Reader Directions**

1.  Use the **Skip to page footer** link near the top or navigate to the very bottom of the page.
2.  Use the **H** key or the **Down Arrow** key to find the footer section labeled **"Assistenza"**.
3.  Under this heading, use the **Tab** key to navigate the links for contact:
    * **"Chiamaci"** (Link for phone contact)
    * **"Entra in chat"** (Link to start a chat session)
    * **"Scrivici"** (Link to write via a contact form or email)
    * **"Reclami e Conciliazione"** (Link for formal complaints)
