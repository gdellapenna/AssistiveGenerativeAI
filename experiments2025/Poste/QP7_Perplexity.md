Yes, it is possible to track the status of a shipment directly from the Poste.it homepage using the "Cerca Spedizioni" (Search Shipments) feature, also known as "Dove Quando."

## Visual Directions
Locate the prominent search bar or "Cerca Spedizioni" field near the top or in the main navigation area. Enter the tracking code (found below the barcode on your receipt, without spaces or hyphens). Click the search icon or press Enter to view status details, including current location, transit updates, and estimated delivery. Add multiple codes separated by commas for batch tracking; expand "Dettagli" for full history and legend explanations.[1][3]

## Screen Reader Directions
Navigate to the top using heading (H1-H2) or landmark keys to find the "search" region or form labeled "Cerca spedizioni o il tuo pacco." Focus lands on the editable text input; type your tracking code (e.g., for raccomandate, pacchi, or international shipments). Tab to the submit button (announced as "Cerca" or magnifying glass), activate with Enter/Space. Results display in a list with expandable sections for states like "In transito" or "Preso in carico," plus options to email/PDF export or SMS alerts.[7][1]

This tracks traceable items like pacchi, raccomandate, assicurate, Posta1, and international up to Italy's border.[3][11][12]
