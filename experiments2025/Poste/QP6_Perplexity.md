Yes, the Poste.it homepage provides ways to log in to personal areas like MyPoste or other services.

## Login Access
A prominent "Accedi con PosteID" or similar login button/link appears, often in the top navigation or header area, directing to secure login pages such as secureloginext.poste.it for entering username and password, or scanning a QR code with the PosteID app for faster access. Users without an account can register via "Registrati" links leading to simplified registration for private individuals or businesses.

## Screen Reader Directions
Use heading navigation (H1-H2) or landmark keys to reach the main header or "navigation" region where the login form or button is announced as "Accedi o Registrati" or "Login with PosteID." Tab to the username field, enter credentials, then tab to the password field and submit button (Enter key). If a QR code option is present, it will be described for app users; fallback to classic username/password mode is available.[1][3][4][10]

This setup supports SPID-enabled PosteID for secure access to online banking, shipments, payments, and personalized services directly from the homepage.[7][11]
