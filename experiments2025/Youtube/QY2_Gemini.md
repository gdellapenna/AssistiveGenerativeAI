### **Screen Reader Directions (How to Interact)**

**1. Reviewing the Search Results**
* Press the **'H'** key to jump through the main **Headings** on the page. You should quickly find headings for the individual video results.
* *Alternatively,* since each video title is a link, press the **'K'** key to jump to the next **Link** until you hear the title of a video you want to watch.

**2. Selecting a Video to Watch**
* When you hear the title of a video you want, press the **'Enter'** or **'Space'** key while the focus is on that link to open and play the video.

**3. Refining Your Search**
* Press the **'E'** key to jump to the **Edit field** (Search box) at the top of the page.
* Once you are in the search box, you can type your new search query.
* Press the **'Tab'** key once to move to the **"Search" button**, and then press **'Enter'** or **'Space'** to perform the new search.
