To watch the first video result ("How To Rank YouTube Videos On The First Page Of Google" by Nate Woodbury) on the search results page (https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=accessibility+web), click on the video's title link, which appears prominently at the top of the main results list. This title is the primary clickable element for each result and leads directly to the video player page where playback begins automatically.

### Directions based on visible content:
- Locate the first video thumbnail and its title text near the top of the page, below the search bar and filters.
- The title is a blue, underlined hyperlink positioned over or next to the video preview image.
- Click directly on the title text to open and start the video.

### Directions for screen reader users:
- Use your screen reader's "Next Heading" or "Next Link" command to reach the first result after the search filters landmark.
- The screen reader announces the video title as a link (e.g., "How To Rank YouTube Videos On The First Page Of Google, link").
- Press Enter when focused on that title link to navigate to the video watch page.
- Standard ARIA labels and focusable elements ensure the title is the main interactive target for playback.[1][11]
