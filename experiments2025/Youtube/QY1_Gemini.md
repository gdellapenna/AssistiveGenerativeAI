You are now on the **YouTube homepage**. This is the main starting place for finding and watching videos.

The page is designed to show you a lot of videos right away, but the most important thing to know is how to find the search bar to look for a specific video.

***

### **Visual Directions (What You See)**

**1. Finding the Search Bar**
* Look at the very top of the page. This area is the main header.
* In the center of the header, you will see a **long, empty box**. This is the **Search** field.
* To the right of this box is a small **magnifying glass icon** on a button.

**2. How to Search**
* **Click** on the long, empty box (the Search field).
* Type in what you want to find, for example, "funny cat videos."
* Then, **click** the **magnifying glass button** to the right of the box to submit your search.

***

### **Screen Reader Directions (How to Interact)**

The most efficient way to start is by navigating directly to the key parts of the page.

**1. Navigating to the Search Bar**
* Press the **'E'** key on your keyboard to jump to the next **Edit field** or **Search box**. The first one you land on will be the main **Search** field.
* *Alternatively,* you can press the **'Insert'** key (or **'Caps Lock'** key for VoiceOver) plus the **'F5'** key to open a list of all form fields on the page. Select the item labeled **"Search"** or **"Search box"** from the list, and your cursor will jump directly there.

**2. How to Search**
* Once your cursor is in the Search field, start typing your search query (e.g., "funny cat videos").
* To activate the search, press the **'Tab'** key once. This will move your focus from the text field to the adjacent **"Search" button**.
* Press the **'Enter'** or **'Space'** key to activate the Search button and view your results.
