That's a great focus, as finding the best places to eat is a key feature of TripAdvisor!

While the homepage doesn't immediately display a list of "best restaurants" without you performing a search, it is **designed** to help you find them through its search functions and categories.

Here is how you would start the process of finding the best restaurants on this page:

### 1. Visual Directions (What you see):

1.  Look directly above the large main search box. You will see several category tabs/buttons.
2.  Find the tab labeled **"Restaurants"** among the choices (usually next to "Hotels" and "Things to Do").
3.  Click the **"Restaurants"** tab. The main search box will change its prompt to encourage you to search for restaurants in a specific location.
4.  Once the search box is focused on restaurants, you would type the **name of the city** you are interested in (e.g., "Paris" or "New York") into the text field and then initiate the search. The results page (on the next screen) is where you will find lists, rankings, and reviews of the "best" in that location.

### 2. Screen Reader Directions (How to navigate):

1.  Navigate to the main search area by pressing **"Tab"** or using the **"F"** key (for form controls).
2.  Use the **left/right arrow keys** to cycle through the search category tabs. Listen for the option: **"Restaurants."**
    * *Action:* Select the **"Restaurants"** radio button/tab. You should hear confirmation like **"Restaurants, selected"** or **"Restaurants, checked."**
3.  Press **"Tab"** once to move into the main search text field.
    * *Listen for:* The screen reader will announce the field with an updated label, such as **"Search Restaurants, edit text."**
4.  Type the **city name** (e.g., "London") into this text field.
5.  Press **"Tab"** one or two more times to find the **"Search" button** and press **"Enter"** or **"Spacebar"** to submit your request and go to the next page of results.
