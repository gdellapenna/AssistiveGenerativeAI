Yes — you *can* log in on the Tripadvisor page. Here’s how:

---

### ✅ Visual instructions

1. On the homepage, look in the top right area (header) for a link labelled **“Join”** or **“Sign In”**.
2. Click that link.
3. A login form will appear — fields will include “Email address” and “Password”.
4. Enter your credentials and click the **“Sign In”** (or similar) button.
5. Once signed in, your user name should appear in the header (instead of “Join”/“Sign In”), and you’ll have access to your account features.

---

### 🎧 Screen-reader instructions

1. After the page loads, press **Tab** repeatedly until you hear something like: “Link: Join” or “Link: Sign In”.
2. When you hear “Link: Sign In”, press **Enter**.
3. You will then hear: “Heading Sign In” (or similar) and then “Edit box: Email address”, followed by “Edit box: Password”.
4. Type your email address, press **Tab** to move to the password field, type your password.
5. Press **Tab** to the “Button: Sign In” or “Button: Continue”, then press **Enter**.
6. After logging in successfully, you’ll hear something like “Link: My Profile” or your user name in the header, indicating you are signed in.
