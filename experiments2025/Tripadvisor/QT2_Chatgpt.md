### Visual description

On the home page you will see:

* A header with a label **“Where to?”** followed by a set of tabs/links: **Hotels**, **Things to Do**, **Restaurants**, **Flights**, **Cruises**. ([Tripadvisor][1])
* A search input (edit box) associated with those categories — you can type in a destination, interest, or activity. ([Tripadvisor][1])
* Under “Things to Do by interest”, categories like *Outdoors*, *Food*, *Culture*, *Water*. ([Tripadvisor][1])
* Below that there are examples of tours/experiences, and also suggestions of cities/destinations you can click. ([Tripadvisor][2])

So in short: you can search for *destinations (places)*, *activities/things to do*, *hotels*, *restaurants*, *flights*, *cruises*.

---

### Screen-reader friendly instructions

1. After the page loads, press **Tab** until you hear something like “Link: Hotels” (or “Tab 1 of X: Hotels”). That’s the first category.
2. Use **arrow keys** (if within a tab list) or continue with **Tab** until you hear “Link: Things to Do”, “Link: Restaurants”, “Link: Flights”, “Link: Cruises”.
3. Once you’ve selected the appropriate category (for example: if you’re looking for restaurants, stop at “Link: Restaurants” and press **Enter**).
4. After entering the category, you will be moved to a search edit box. You will hear something like “Edit box: Search” or “Search field”. Use your keyboard to type your query—this could be a city name, an attraction, a type of food, etc.
5. Press **Enter** (or sometimes Tab to the “Search” button then Enter) to start the search.
6. After results load, you can use headings (H key) or list navigation to jump between sections such as “Experiences travelers love”, “Iconic places you need to see”, etc.
