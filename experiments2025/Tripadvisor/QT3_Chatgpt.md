2. **Main header navigation** — a row of links near the top of the page:

   * Discover
   * Trips
   * Review
   * More
     These give high-level navigation across the site.
3. **Category tabs beneath “Where to?”** — a menu of category links for search:

   * Hotels
   * Things to Do
   * Restaurants
   * Flights
   * Cruises
     This acts like a menu for what you want to search.

### Screen-reader friendly instructions

Here’s how you find and use these menu items using a screen reader:

1. After the page loads, start by pressing **Tab** until you hear something like “Link: Skip to main content”. That will skip past the header if you want.
2. If you choose to stay in the header, continue pressing **Tab**. You will hear the main navigation links: “Link: Discover”, “Link: Trips”, “Link: Review”, “Link: More”. You can press **Enter** when you hear the one you want.
3. After the header nav, press **Tab** until you hear something like “Link: Hotels” (or the first of the category tabs). Then you’ll hear “Link: Things to Do”, “Link: Restaurants”, “Link: Flights”, “Link: Cruises”. You can arrow-right/arrow-left if it’s a tab list, or just keep pressing Tab. Press **Enter** on the tab/category you want before entering your search.
4. Then you’ll reach an edit box labelled “Search” (or something like “Edit box: Search”). You can type your destination or interest there. After typing, press **Enter** (or Tab to the “Search” button and then press Enter).
5. After search results load, you can navigate by headings. Press **H** to move through main sections: you’ll hear headings like “Find things to do by interest”, “Experiences travelers love”, etc. Within those, the links will have descriptive names you can activate.
6. If you want the footer navigation, press **Ctrl+End** (or a lot of Tabs) to move to bottom and then you’ll tab through links like “About Tripadvisor”, “Press”, etc. Each will be announced as “Link: …”. Use Enter to follow them.
7. To change region/currency, perform Tab until you find something like “Button: Change region” or “Link: Choose country/currency”. Press Enter, then navigate the list (arrow keys or Tab) to select your desired region/currency.
8. To sign in or join, Tab until you hear “Link: Join” or “Link: Sign in” at the top. Press Enter to open the form, then tab through username/password fields and buttons following typical form navigation.
