On the Tripadvisor homepage, you can search for several main categories of travel-related content. The search functionality allows you to look for:

- Hotels and other accommodations
- Things to Do (such as tours, attractions, and activities)
- Restaurants
- Flights
- Vacation Rentals

Each category lets you enter specific details like location, dates, and preferences to narrow down results. For example, you can search for hotels in a city for particular dates, or find restaurants nearby. There are also filters available to refine your search by traveler rankings, price, amenities, and more.

So, essentially, you can search for places to stay, eat, travel experiences, and flight options all from this page.

For screen reader use:
- When the page loads, the main navigation categories labeled "Hotels," "Things to Do," "Restaurants," "Flights," and "Vacation Rentals" will be announced.
- You can navigate using the Tab key or arrow keys to the search area corresponding to the category of interest.
- Type in your destination or relevant keywords, then use associated buttons (e.g., "Search") to initiate a search.
- Filters appear as selectable options after initiating a search to narrow down results further.

This search capability allows comprehensive planning of trips directly from the homepage.[1]
