On the Tripadvisor homepage, you can search for a wide variety of travel-related topics using the main search bar and navigation menu.

---

### 🖥️ **Visual Instructions (for sighted users)**

You can search for:

- **Destinations**: Type a city, country, or region into the large “Where to?” search box near the top.
- **Hotels**: Click the “Hotels” tab in the top navigation bar to search for accommodations.
- **Things to Do**: Click “Things to Do” to find tours, attractions, and activities.
- **Restaurants**: Click “Restaurants” to explore dining options in a location.
- **Flights**: Click “Flights” to compare airline tickets.
- **Cruises**: Click “Cruises” to browse cruise options.
- **Vacation Rentals**: Click “Vacation Rentals” to find homes and apartments for rent.
- **Forums**: Scroll down and click “Travel Forums” to ask questions or read advice from other travelers.

Each of these options leads to a dedicated search page where you can enter more specific criteria.

---

### 🗣️ **Screen Reader Instructions (for blind users)**

You can search for:

- **Destinations**:
  - Use heading navigation to locate the “Where to?” search field.
  - Type a city or country name and press Enter.

- **Hotels, Restaurants, Flights, Cruises, Things to Do**:
  - Use heading or link navigation to reach the top navigation bar.
  - Tab through links labeled “Hotels”, “Restaurants”, “Flights”, “Cruises”, “Things to Do”.
  - Press Enter on any of these to open their search page.

- **Vacation Rentals**:
  - Navigate to the link labeled “Vacation Rentals” in the top menu and press Enter.

- **Travel Forums**:
  - Use heading navigation to reach the footer.
  - Tab to the link labeled “Travel Forums” and press Enter.
