Yes, there is a clear way to log in on this page.

The login function is located in the primary navigation area at the very top of the page.

### 1. Visual Directions (What you see):

1.  Look at the **top right corner** of the page header.
2.  You will find a button or link labeled **"Sign in."**
3.  Clicking this link will take you to the login page where you can enter your account details.

### 2. Screen Reader Directions (How to navigate):

The "Sign in" element is an important interactive control, often accessible directly.

1.  Start by pressing **"Tab"** repeatedly from the top of the page (after passing the main search bar) or use the quick navigation keys.
2.  The fastest way is often to use the **"B"** key (for Button) or the **"L"** key (for Link) repeatedly until you hear the screen reader announce: **"Link, Sign in"** or **"Button, Sign in."**
3.  Press **"Enter"** or **"Spacebar"** on this element to activate it and proceed to the login screen.
