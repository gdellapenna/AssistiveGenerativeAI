To search for a specific hotel on the Tripadvisor homepage, you’ll use the main search bar or the “Hotels” section. Here’s how to do it:

---

### 🖥️ **Visual Instructions (for sighted users)**

1. **Locate the Search Bar**:
   - Near the top center of the page, find the large white search box labeled **“Where to?”**.

2. **Type the Hotel Name**:
   - Click inside the box and type the full name of the hotel you’re looking for (e.g., “Hotel Danieli Venice”).

3. **Select the Hotel from Suggestions**:
   - As you type, a dropdown will appear with matching results.
   - Click on the correct hotel name from the list.

4. **View Hotel Page**:
   - You’ll be taken to the hotel’s page with reviews, photos, prices, and booking options.

---

### 🗣️ **Screen Reader Instructions (for blind users)**

1. **Navigate to the Search Field**:
   - Use **form field navigation** to locate the input labeled **“Where to?”**.

2. **Enter the Hotel Name**:
   - Type the full name of the hotel (e.g., “Hotel Danieli Venice”) and pause briefly.

3. **Interact with Suggestions**:
   - Use **arrow keys** to move through the dropdown list of suggestions.
   - When you hear the correct hotel name, press **Enter** to select it.

4. **Access Hotel Page**:
   - The page will load with detailed information about the hotel, including reviews and booking options.
