Below are **step-by-step directions** for searching for a **specific hotel** on the Tripadvisor **home page** you gave
→ [https://www.tripadvisor.com/](https://www.tripadvisor.com/)

First you’ll get **visual (sighted-user) instructions**, then **screen-reader instructions**.

---

# ✅ VISUAL INSTRUCTIONS

1. On the home page, look near the top for the large text **“Where to?”**
2. Just below it you will see a row of category tabs such as:

   * **Hotels**
   * Things to Do
   * Restaurants
   * Flights
     …etc.
3. Click **Hotels**.
4. Below the tabs, you will see a **search box** with a placeholder such as **“Search”**.
5. Click inside the search box, and type the **name of the hotel** you want (e.g., “Hotel Example Rome”).
6. Press **Enter**, or click the **Search button / magnifying glass icon**.
7. Tripadvisor will load a results page listing matching hotels for you to select.

---

# ✅ SCREEN-READER INSTRUCTIONS

1. After the page loads, press **Tab** until you reach the category tabs.
   You will hear items like:

   * “Link: Hotels”
   * “Link: Things to Do”
   * “Link: Restaurants”
2. When you hear **“Hotels (link)”**, press **Enter**.
3. Press **Tab** until you reach the **search edit field**.
   You will hear something like:

   * “Search, edit text”
4. Type the **hotel name** (e.g., “Hotel Example Rome”).
5. Press **Enter** to search.
   If pressing Enter does not start the search, Tab until you hear a search button (possibly “Search” or “Magnifying glass”) and press **Enter**.
6. Your results page will load. Navigate through:

   * Headings (press **H**)
   * Links (press **Tab**)
     Each result entry will usually announce:
   * Hotel name (link)
   * Rating
   * Number of reviews
   * Location

Press Enter on the hotel result you want to open.
