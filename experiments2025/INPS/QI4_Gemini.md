Yes, the INPS homepage provides a clear pathway to finding contact information and various assistance methods.

This information is primarily located under two linked sections, often found in the footer or a dedicated administrative area:

### **1. Sedi e Contatti (Locations and Contacts)**

This section details both physical locations and direct contact channels:

| Italian Label | English Translation | Contact Type |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Sedi** | **Locations** | Physical offices and representatives. |
| *— Ricerca Sede* | *— Search Location* | (Link to find a specific INPS office) |
| *— Elenco PEC* | *— PEC List* | (Certified Electronic Mail addresses) |
| *— Punti INPS* | *— INPS Points* | (Service points) |
| *— Patronati* | *— Patronage Organizations* | (Organizations that assist citizens with INPS practices) |
| **Contatti** | **Contacts** | Direct communication methods. |
| *— PEC* | *— Certified Electronic Mail* | (Email for official communications) |
| *— Contact Center Multicanale* | *— Multi-channel Contact Center* | (Details for phone and other contact services) |
| *— INPS Risponde* | *— INPS Responds* | (Likely a link to an online help/response service) |

***

### **2. Assistenza (Assistance)**

This section provides links for users needing technical or procedural help, which can often substitute for direct contact:

* **FAQ – Domande frequenti** (FAQs – Frequently Asked Questions)
* **Supporto online** (Online Support)
* **Che cos'è MyINPS?** (What is MyINPS?)
* **Le credenziali per accedere ai servizi** (Credentials to access services)
* **SPID - Sistema Pubblico di Identità Digitale** (Public Digital Identity System information)
