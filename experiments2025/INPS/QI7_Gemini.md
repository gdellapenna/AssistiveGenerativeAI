Yes, there are multiple ways indicated on the homepage to log in or access personalized services.

The primary log-in link is simply labeled **"Accedi" (Access/Log in)** and is highly visible, usually found in the header of the page.

Additionally, within the main menu sections, such as **"Pensione e Previdenza"** and **"Lavoro,"** there is a direct tool link:

* **Accedi a MyINPS** (Access MyINPS)

The **Assistenza (Assistance)** section also references the required digital credentials for access:

* **Le credenziali per accedere ai servizi** (Credentials to access services)
* **SPID - Sistema Pubblico di Identità Digitale** (Public Digital Identity System)
* **CIE - Carta d'Identità Elettronica** (Electronic Identity Card)
* **CNS - Carta Nazionale dei Servizi** (National Services Card)

In summary, you would use the **"Accedi"** link or **"Accedi a MyINPS"** link and then use one of the specified digital identity credentials (SPID, CIE, or CNS) to log in.
