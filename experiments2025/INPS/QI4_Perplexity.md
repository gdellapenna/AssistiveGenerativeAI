Yes, the INPS homepage explains how to contact INPS through a dedicated section titled Contatti INPS.

This section details multiple contact options, including:
- Contact Center Multicanale, active Monday to Friday 8-20 and Saturday 8-14, with links to deepen information.
- Phone numbers: 803 164 (free from landline) and 06 164 164 (from mobile, charged per carrier tariff).
- INPS Risponde, for sending requests for regulatory clarifications and information.
- Sedi INPS, listing INPS offices and Patronato offices, with a tool to find nearby locations.

The explanation emphasizes various physical, digital, and phone channels to choose the most suitable one.[11]
