Yes — the INPS home page gives you **two main ways to search for a specific argument or service**.  

---

## 👀 Visual steps (sighted navigation)

1. **Top search option (“Ricerca”):**  
   - At the very top of the page header, next to “Accedi,” there is a link labeled **“Ricerca.”**  
   - Click it, and a search field will appear.  
   - Type the name of the service or topic (for example, “pensione” or “bonus famiglia”).  
   - Press Enter or click the search button to see results.

2. **Guided tool (“Qual è la tua necessità?”):**  
   - Scroll down until you find the section titled **“Qual è la tua necessità?”**  
   - Here you’ll see two selectors:  
     - **“Chi sei?”** (choose your profile, e.g., cittadino, impresa, pensionato).  
     - **“Quale argomento ti interessa?”** (choose the topic, e.g., pensione, lavoro, sostegni).  
   - After selecting both, the page will show the relevant services.  

---

## 🎧 Screen reader steps (non-visual navigation)

1. **Using the search (“Ricerca”):**  
   - Navigate to the top of the page.  
   - Tab until you hear the link labeled **“Ricerca.”**  
   - Press Enter to activate it.  
   - Focus will move to a search input field. Type your query (e.g., “pensione”).  
   - Press Enter to submit. The results page will load with links matching your query.

2. **Using the guided tool (“Qual è la tua necessità?”):**  
   - Navigate by headings until you reach **“Qual è la tua necessità?”**  
   - Tab to the selector labeled **“Chi sei? Seleziona.”** Press Space or Enter to open, arrow through the options, and press Enter to choose.  
   - Tab to the selector labeled **“Quale argomento ti interessa? Seleziona.”** Open it, arrow through the topics, and press Enter to select.  
   - After both are chosen, Tab to the action button or link that appears, and press Enter to view the suggested services.

---

✅ So you can either **use the search bar at the top** or **use the guided tool in the middle of the page** to find a specific argument or service.
