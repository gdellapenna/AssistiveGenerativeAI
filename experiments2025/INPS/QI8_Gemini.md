Yes, you have two primary ways to search for a specific argument or service on the INPS page:

### **1. Using the Main Search Bar**

The most direct way is to use the **search bar** functionality. While the exact location can vary slightly, it is typically highly visible near the top or corner of the page.

* Look for a magnifying glass icon or a field labeled **"Ricerca"** (Search) or similar.
* Type your specific keywords (e.g., "NASpI," "Assegno Unico," "pensione anticipata").

### **2. Navigating the Thematic Categories**

The page is highly organized by major thematic areas, which you can use to narrow down your search:

* **Pensione e Previdenza** (Pension and Social Security)
* **Lavoro** (Work/Employment)
* **Sostegni, Sussidi e Indennità** (Support, Subsidies, and Allowances)
* **Imprese e Liberi Professionisti** (Companies and Freelance Professionals)

You can click on the main category that matches your topic of interest, and the sub-menu will guide you to a more specific service or argument.

For instance, to find the **NASpI** unemployment benefit, you would typically:
1. Click on **Lavoro** (Work/Employment).
2. Select **Disoccupazione** (Unemployment) from the sub-menu.
