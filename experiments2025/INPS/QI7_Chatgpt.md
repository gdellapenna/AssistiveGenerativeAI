## 🔐 How to log in

### Sighted navigation (what you’d see / click)

* On the home page of INPS, you should find a button or link labelled **“Accedi a MyINPS”** (or just “Accedi”) near the top right of the page. ([INPS][2])
* If you click that button, you will be taken to a login page for “MyINPS” — the personal area. ([INPS][2])
* On the login page, you can choose one of several authentication methods: **SPID** (digital identity), **Carta d'Identità Elettronica (CIE)**, or **Carta Nazionale dei Servizi (CNS)**. ([INPS][2])
* After authenticating with one of those methods, you will be logged in and redirected to your personal area (“MyINPS”). ([INPS][3])

---

## 🧑‍🦯 How to log in using a screen reader (step-by-step)

1. When the homepage loads, navigate via links or by heading navigation until you find a link labeled **“Accedi a MyINPS”** or **“Accedi”** near the top of the page.
2. Activate that link (e.g. press Enter) — this opens the login page.
3. On the login page, your screen reader will list available authentication options — you should hear “SPID — link”, “CIE — link”, “CNS — link” (or similar).
4. Move to the option you prefer (e.g. SPID) and activate it.
5. Follow the authentication procedure: for example, if using SPID, select your identity provider when prompted, enter your credentials (or approve via smartphone/app), as guided.
6. After successful authentication, you will be redirected to your personal area. The screen reader will announce the “Dashboard” or “Bacheca” — meaning you are logged in.
