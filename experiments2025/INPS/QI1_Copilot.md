### Current page status

I’ve analyzed the current live version of the INPS home page at https://www.inps.it/. The page is in Italian, with a prominent top area for “Accedi” (login) and “Ricerca” (search), main navigation categories, news items, featured services, and a footer containing contact options and legal links. A cookie preferences panel is available and can be opened via an icon at the bottom left or the keyboard shortcut Alt+C. The site marks itself as the “Sito ufficiale di INPS (Istituto Nazionale Previdenza Sociale).”

- **Popular services:** Cards/links like **Fascicolo previdenziale del cittadino**, **Pensami – Simulatore scenari pensionistici**, **Cedolino della pensione**, and others.  
- **Guided tool:** “Qual è la tua necessità?” with profile and topic selectors (“Chi sei?” and “Quale argomento ti interessa?”), leading to relevant services.  
- **MyINPS:** Promotional block with a button **Accedi a MyINPS** for a personalized area.  
- **Contacts:** “Contatti INPS” section listing **Contact Center Multicanale** with hours, numbers (**803 164** fixed-line free; **06 164 164** mobile, paid), **INPS Risponde**, and **Sedi INPS** with a link to “Trova le sedi INPS vicine a te.”  
- **Footer:** Repeats main categories, accessibility and legal links (e.g., **Dichiarazione accessibilità**, **Note Legali**, **Privacy**), **Impostazioni dei cookie**, app, and organizational details (address, VAT).  
- **Cookie controls:** A “Uso dei cookie” panel with toggles for “Cookie tecnici” and “Cookie di statistica,” buttons **Accetta tutti**, **Rifiuta i cookie non tecnici**, **Salva impostazioni**. You can open preferences via a bottom-left icon or press **Alt+C**.

### Common actions you might need

#### Access the login (Accedi)

- **Visual steps:**
  - **Accedi:** At the top of the page header, locate and click the **Accedi** link to start the login process. This is near the “ITA” indicator and search.

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Find Accedi link:** Press Alt+1 if your reader jumps to the top, or navigate to the top navigation. Use Tab until you land on the link labeled “Accedi,” then press Enter.

#### Use the site search (Ricerca)

- **Visual steps:**
  - **Ricerca:** In the top header, click **Ricerca**. A search input will be available; type your query and submit (often via Enter or a search button).

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Search control:** Tab to the link or control labeled “Ricerca,” activate with Enter. When focus moves to the search field, type your query and press Enter to execute the search. If a button is present, Tab to it and press Enter.

#### Open a popular service (e.g., Cedolino della pensione)

- **Visual steps:**
  - **I più utilizzati:** Scroll to the “I più utilizzati” section. Click the **Cedolino della pensione** card/link to access that service. Other options include “Fascicolo previdenziale del cittadino” and “Pensami.”

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Navigate by heading:** Jump to the heading “I più utilizzati.” Use Tab to move through the links. When you hear “Cedolino della pensione,” press Enter to open it. Similarly, activate other listed services as needed.

#### Use “Qual è la tua necessità?” guided tool

- **Visual steps:**
  - **Selectors:** Find the section titled “Qual è la tua necessità?” Select an option under **Chi sei?** (the profile selector), then pick an option under **Quale argomento ti interessa?** After both are set, proceed to the displayed services or an action to access them.

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Selectors via keyboard:** Navigate to “Qual è la tua necessità?” Use Tab to reach the control labeled “Chi sei? Seleziona,” press Space/Enter to open, arrow through options, and press Enter to select. Repeat for “Quale argomento ti interessa? Seleziona.” Then Tab to the action/control that lets you access the suggested services and press Enter.

#### Access MyINPS

- **Visual steps:**
  - **Button:** Locate the “Accedi a MyINPS” block and click the **Accedi a MyINPS** button to go to your personal area.

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Activate link/button:** Find the heading “Accedi a MyINPS.” Tab until you reach the button/link “Accedi a MyINPS,” then press Enter.

#### Find contact options and phone numbers

- **Visual steps:**
  - **Contatti INPS:** Scroll to the “Contatti INPS” section. Note the hours for **Contact Center Multicanale** (Mon–Fri 8–20; Sat 8–14). The numbers are **803 164** (free from landline) and **06 164 164** (mobile, paid). Click “Approfondisci” for more details, “INPS Risponde” to send requests, or “Trova le sedi INPS vicine a te” to find offices.

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Headings and links:** Navigate to the heading “Contatti INPS.” Read the hours and phone numbers. Tab to “Approfondisci,” “INPS Risponde,” or “Trova le sedi INPS vicine a te,” and press Enter on the one you need. The numbers and descriptions are text; links provide further actions.

#### Manage cookie preferences

- **Visual steps:**
  - **Open panel:** Click the cookie preferences icon at the bottom-left of the page to open “Uso dei cookie.”  
  - **Set preferences:** Toggle **Cookie tecnici** and **Cookie di statistica** as desired. Click **Accetta tutti**, **Rifiuta i cookie non tecnici**, or **Salva impostazioni** to apply.

- **Screen reader steps:**
  - **Open via keyboard:** Press **Alt+C** to open the cookie preferences panel.  
  - **Adjust and save:** Tab to the toggles for “Cookie tecnici” and “Cookie di statistica,” use Space to change their state, then Tab to the buttons “Accetta tutti,” “Rifiuta i cookie non tecnici,” or “Salva impostazioni,” and press Enter.
