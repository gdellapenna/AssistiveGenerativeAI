## 👀 Visual description of “Lavoro” submenu

When you hover or click on **“Lavoro”** in the main navigation bar, the submenu expands and shows the following groups of services:

- **Congedi, permessi e certificati**  
  - Services to request leave, permits, and submit certificates.  
  - Includes maternity/paternity leave, sick leave, and related documentation.  

- **Disoccupazione**  
  - Services to request unemployment benefits and economic support.  
  - Covers NASpI and other unemployment-related allowances.  

- **Contratti e rapporti di lavoro**  
  - Services to manage employment relationships or resignations.  
  - Options for agricultural, domestic, or occasional work contracts.  

- **Contributi per dipendenti e collaboratori**  
  - Services to pay contributions for employees or collaborators.  

- **Contributi figurativi / valorizzazione lavoro e studio**  
  - Services to request figurative contributions (e.g., military service, civil service, study periods).  

- **Fondi di garanzia**  
  - Services related to guarantee funds for workers.  

Each of these submenu items usually leads to a dedicated page listing the available services in that category.
