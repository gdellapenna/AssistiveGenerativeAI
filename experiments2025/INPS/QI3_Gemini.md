1.  **Locate the Menu:** Use the heading navigation keys (usually **H**) to jump to the major section heading labeled **"Lavoro"**.
2.  **Explore Service Categories:** Use the **Tab** key to move down through the list of links that start with `Congedi, permessi e certificati` and continue down to `Concorsi, interpelli e reclutamenti INPS`.
3.  **Access Tools:** After the last service category link, you will encounter a dedicated sub-section labeled **"Strumenti"** (Tools). Continue using the **Tab** key to access and activate the tool links, such as **"Accedi a MyINPS"**.
