The "Lavoro" menu on the INPS main website includes several submenu items related to managing employment relationships and associated services. These typically cover areas such as:

- Management of employment contracts including different types.
- Services related to leaves, permits, certificates, and economic supports.
- Unemployment benefits and economic contributions.
- Contract variations, dismissals, and specific work sector regulations.
- Specialized portals related to particular types of employment such as domestic work or freelance jobs.
- Access to tools for consulting, modifying, or requesting service operations linked to employment.

Specifically, the submenu items under "Lavoro" are organized by themes like:

- Contratti e rapporti di lavoro (Contracts and employment relationships),
- Gestione dei congedi e permessi (Management of leaves and permits),
- Indennità e sostegni economici (Allowances and economic supports),
- Servizi per disoccupati (Services for unemployed),
- Cassetto previdenziale (Social security drawer) for employers,
- Portale dei Pagamenti (Payments portal),
- Specific areas for domestic workers or occasional work.

These submenu items enable users to directly access the relevant employment management services tailored to their profile or specific needs. The structure is designed to simplify navigation and provide efficient access to essential employment and labor-related INPS services.

For a screen reader, these submenu items appear as grouped links under the "Lavoro" heading/menu, providing a clear path to detailed service sections and tools.[1][2][3]
